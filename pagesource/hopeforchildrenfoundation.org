Every victim including Simone Biles, and the other Olympic gymnastic victims who became survivors, are truly our heroes.</p>

<h3>Learn how devastating child abuse is in America.</h3>
<p><iframe src="https://player.vimeo.com/video/178237050?title=0&amp;portrait=0" width="400" height="300" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen"></iframe></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14px;">Abused children need all the support possible to recover from trauma experienced by the hands of their abusers.</span> 30% of child abusers have experienced abuse on their own bodies during childhood. Child abusers need help with learning how to break the cycles of violence.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong>Hope For Children Foundation, an Acknowledged Leader in America,</strong> <strong>i</strong></span><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">ntending to reduce cruelty and violence against children, adults and you. </span></strong></p>
<p><strong><span style="font-size: 16px;">FBI Report &#8211; Effects of child abuse are devastating to children. </span></strong>About 97% percent of abused children reported to the legal system are not protected by the legal system. This fact was calculated from input received by the FBI from law enforcement agencies within each state throughout the <strong>United States</strong>. <strong>Hope For Children Foundation</strong> spot checked this statistic and unfortunately, this national average is correct. What will you do within your family to help <strong>stop child abuse</strong>?</p>
<p>Hopefully child abuse does not exist in your family, although what if <strong>child abuse</strong> does exist in your family? You can get help to stop it. Yes, we want to help you and your <strong>family</strong> to live an abuse free life. It is possible. It takes work and commitment, and with the right help, it is possible. This <strong>children&#8217;s charity</strong> provides free online training intending to help you and your<strong> family</strong> achieve living an abuse free life, <strong>prevent child abuse</strong> and <strong>stop child abuse</strong> in your family.</p>
<p>Hope For Children Foundation uses the power of the Internet, via our Web site https://hopeforchildrenfoundation.org/ to enable a local, national and global presence. We extend an invitation to you, join us please and our mission to help <strong>prevent child abuse</strong>. Start by accessing our free online training to help <strong>stop child abuse</strong>. Learn about true love, kindness, respect, and a call to justice for precious innocent <strong>children</strong>, while building character within your daily actions. Implement what you learn into action by reducing child abuse. Support our social media and share with your family and friends. Let them know we are one of many great <strong>charities for children</strong>. We do not charge for this training, it is our gift to you, your family, and friends. Should you feel you have received a benefit from the training and have a few extra dollars to help continue our mission, then please donate by clicking on our donate button on this site, or mail a check to the address listed within this article.</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 16px;"><br />
Child abuse includes but is not limited to: </span>All violence against children, bullying, domestic violence, child neglect, physical abuse of <strong>children</strong>, strangulation, starvation, indecency with a <strong>child</strong>, sexual assault, incest, rape <strong>victims</strong> and murder. <strong>Child abuse </strong><strong>statistics</strong> show most <strong>children</strong> are sexually and/or physically abused by people they know and trust. <strong>Hope For Children Foundation</strong> encourages you, your family and friends to educate yourself about these issues by reviewing the FREE video training available through our Youtube, <strong>HopeForChildrenFndTX</strong> Channel or Click on Training on this Web site <strong>https://hopeforchildrenfoundation.org/ </strong>Learn how to <strong>report child abuse</strong> and perhaps help save the lives of your <strong>children</strong>.</p>

<h5><strong>Hope For Children Foundation a Leader in America</strong></h5>
<p><strong>Hope For Children Foundation is </strong><strong>an acknowledged leader in America</strong> involved with the fight against <strong>child abuse</strong> in the <strong>United States</strong> and abroad. <strong>Children</strong> are very vulnerable and need the nation&#8217;s help, especially since <strong>child abuse</strong> is everyone&#8217;s business! We encourage <strong>families</strong> to take steps and learn how to reduce <strong>violent crimes</strong> within their own<strong> family</strong>. It just takes one brave person in a <strong>family</strong> to begin reducing violence in the home. Please be the voice in your <strong>family</strong> to help your <strong>family</strong> members. Remember violence is a learned behavior. Non-violence is also a learned behavior. We all must value the relationships within our own <strong>families</strong>. Please start now and allow <strong>Hope For Children Foundation</strong> to help you and your <strong>family</strong>. We are one of many <strong>children&#8217;s charities</strong>, although our services are unique. Our training is free, should you find you or your <strong>family</strong> have gained value from our <strong>online training</strong>, please donate to further our mission. <p><span style="font-size: 16px;">Helping <strong>children</strong> is the goal of <strong>Hope For Children Foundation</strong>. We are here to provide guidance, support, suggestions, answers and education, helping to find the right path of action for protecting your <strong>child</strong> and yourself from abusers. </span>Full awareness of the immediate needs of crime victims will allow first responders including state and federal <strong>law enforcement officers</strong>, judges and court personnel to make qualified decisions from the start. Then followed by the &#8220;right actions&#8221; hopefully will bring about better protection for victims.</p>
<p>The state and federal <strong>law enforcement training</strong> is available through our FREE <strong>video training</strong> to better protect <strong>children</strong> and other citizens. It is time for <strong>victims</strong>, their <strong>families</strong>, neighbors and the general public to receive this knowledge. United we can take steps to help<strong> stop child abuse</strong>! We also provide training on The <strong>Violence Against Women</strong> Act. These federal laws provide excellent protection for <strong>children</strong>, women, and men. It is the goal of <strong>Hope For Children Foundation</strong> to <strong>prevent cruelty to children</strong>. We provide free seminars and <strong>online video training</strong> to the general public, and to professionals who are charged with <strong>protecting children</strong> and adults against <strong>sexual assault</strong> and <strong>domestic violence</strong>. Trainees have received the education courses well.<span style="color: #000000;"><strong> </strong></span></p>
