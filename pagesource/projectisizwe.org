<div class="service col-md-4">
<div class="roll-icon-box">
<div class="content">
<h3>
</h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><img class="wp-image-2687 aligncenter" src="//www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/WSA-Awards-1.png" width="162" height="108" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Tshwane’s <strong>TshWi-Fi.co.za</strong> content portal has been chosen as a global <strong>World Summit Award winner</strong> in the category Government and Citizen Engagement. The award is initiated in the framework of the United Nations in an effort to help bridge the world&#8217;s digital divide. The WSA highlights and celebrates digital content that improves society, focusing on local content with global relevance. <a href="http://www.projectisizwe.org/index.php/2017/03/24/world-summit-award-winners/"><strong>Read more here.</strong></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="service col-md-4">
<div class="roll-icon-box">
<div class="content">
<h3>
</h3>
<p><img class="wp-image-2696 size-full aligncenter" src="//www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/bookmark.png" width="160" height="160" srcset="//www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/bookmark.png 160w, //www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/03/bookmark-150x150.png 150w" sizes="(max-width: 160px) 100vw, 160px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The <strong>City of Tshwane</strong> and <strong>Project </strong>Isizwe’s collaborative <strong>TshWi-Fi</strong> deployment walked away with two top achievements at the prestigious <strong>2017 IAB Bookmark Awards</strong>. TshWi-Fi.co.za, the unlimited access content portal, received a Gold in the Public Service and NPO Platforms category. TshWi-Fi TV, the citizen journalism digital video project, proudly accepted a Silver award in the Online Video Channels Category. <strong><a href="http://www.projectisizwe.org/index.php/2017/03/24/city-tshwanes-tshwi-fi-takes-gold-silver-2017-bookmark-awards/">Read more here.</a></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="content">
<h3>
</h3>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-2615" src="//www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/fire-awards-winner.png" width="226" height="150" srcset="//www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/fire-awards-winner.png 377w, //www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/fire-awards-winner-300x199.png 300w, //www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/fire-awards-winner-230x153.png 230w, //www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/fire-awards-winner-350x232.png 350w, //www.projectisizwe.org/wp-content/uploads/2016/12/fire-awards-winner-272x182.png 272w" sizes="(max-width: 226px) 100vw, 226px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>TshWi-Fi TV</strong>, Tshwane&#8217;s video-on-demand citizen journalism project, is a winner in the 2016 FIRE Africa Awards for being an internet service that promotes social inclusion, strengthens democracy and allows for the exercise of rights and freedoms. This innovative collaboration between the <strong>City of Tshwane</strong> and <strong>Project Isizwe</strong> delivers hyper-local education and entertainment videos to Tshwane’s Free Wi-Fi users.<br />
</span></p>

<b>Project Isizwe</b> is an award winning, non-profit organisation which aims to bring the internet to people across South Africa, by facilitating the roll-out of Free WiFi for public spaces in low income communities, with a core focus on connectedness for the purpose of education.
