        <td width="420">
          Metapedia is an electronic encyclopedia about culture, art, science, philosophy and politics.<br />
          <br />
          The word 'Metapedia' is derived from two concepts from classical greek:  'meta' that means 'outside' or 'beyond'; and 'enkyklios paideia' that  means 'encyclopedia'. The name has a dual symbolic meaning:<br />
          <ul>
            <li>            Metapedia sets its focus on topics that usually are not covered in — i.e. that falls <em>outside</em> of — mainstream encyclopedias.<br />
              <br />
            </li>
            <li>Metapedia has a <em>metapolitical</em> purpose,  to influence the mainstream debate, culture and historical view.</li>
          </ul>          
          <p>            The project is still in its early stages, but the database is growing  every day and you are heartily welcome to contribute to the growth of  this valuable and unique encyclopedia.<br />
              <br />
