      <header>
                    <h2 class="node__title node-title"><a href="/home">What is the Millennium Mathematics Project?</a></h2>
            
      
          </header>
  
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><div class="cam-column11">
<div class="cam-content-container">
<article class="cam-horizontal-teaser cam-teaser"><p>The  Millennium Mathematics Project (MMP) is a maths education and outreach initiative for ages 3 to 19 and the general public. The MMP is a collaboration between the Faculties of Mathematics and Education at the University of Cambridge, and is active nationally and internationally. Our focus is on increasing mathematical understanding, confidence and enjoyment, developing problem-solving skills, and promoting creative and imaginative approaches to maths.</p>
<p>The project consists of a family of complementary programmes, including the very successful <a href="http://nrich.maths.org">NRICH</a> website, <a href="http://plus.maths.org">Plus</a> online mathematics magazine, and face-to-face work with schools and the public. In the 2016/17 school year our online mathematical resources attracted nearly 12 million visits from users worldwide and over 41 million page views, while around 12,000 students and more than 5,000 teachers were involved in our face-to-face activities and events.</p>

<p></p></article></div>
<hr class="cam-teaser-divider" /><div class="cam-column12">
<div class="cam-column6">
<article class="cam-content-container cam-side-padding cam-sub-column-left-border"><a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://nrich.maths.org/public/index.php"><img class="cam-scale-with-grid cam-float-left" src="https://maths.org/sites/mmp.maths.org/files/tt_MathsSport_boy-showjump_0.JPG" alt="Image: Student working on NRICH activity. (c) University of Cambridge / Nadia Baker" /></a>
<div class="cam-content-container cam-vertical-teaser-txt">
<h3 class="cam-teaser-title"><a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://nrich.maths.org/public/index.php">NRICH</a></h3>
<p>
The <a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://nrich.maths.org/public/index.php">NRICH</a> website publishes thousands of free resources designed to challenge, inspire and engage ages 3 to 19. Teachers' resources support planning and help teachers promote rich mathematical thinking in the classroom. NRICH resources focus on problem-solving and take a low-threshold high-ceiling approach, building students' confidence, mathematical reasoning, thinking skills and ability to take the initiative.
</p>
</div>
<p></p></article></div>
<div class="cam-column6">
<article class="cam-content-container cam-side-padding  cam-sub-column-left-border cam-sub-column-right-border"><a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://plus.maths.org/"><img class="cam-scale-with-grid cam-float-left" src="https://maths.org/sites/mmp.maths.org/files/tt_infinitypostcard.jpg" alt="Plus postcard" /></a>
<div class="cam-content-container cam-vertical-teaser-txt">
<h3 class="cam-teaser-title"><a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://plus.maths.org/">Plus</a></h3>
<p>
<a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://plus.maths.org/">Plus</a> is a free online maths magazine aimed at the interested and intelligent general reader, including older students (16+). Lively, accessible and in-depth articles and podcasts explore all aspects of maths, ranging from what string theory predicts about hidden dimensions to mathematics in medicine. Meanwhile news items uncover the hidden maths behind media headlines and report news from the world of research.
</p>
</div>
<p></p></article></div>
</div>
<hr class="cam-teaser-divider" /><div class="cam-column12">
<div class="cam-column6">
<article class="cam-content-container cam-side-padding cam-sub-column-left-border"><br /><a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://maths.org/roadshow"><img class="cam-scale-with-grid cam-float-left" src="/sites/mmp.maths.org/files/tt_car_Nine_colours_finished-cube_rect-crop.jpg" alt="Image: FMSP/Phil Ball (MMP Hands-On Maths Roadshow activity - Nine Colours)" /></a>
<div class="cam-content-container cam-vertical-teaser-txt">
<h3 class="cam-teaser-title"><a onclick="window.open(this.href,'_blank');return false;" href="http://maths.org/roadshow">Visits to schools</a></h3>
<p>
We visit schools all over the UK and abroad with the <a href="http://www.mmp.maths.org/roadshow">Hands-On Maths Roadshow</a>, presenting creative methods of exploring mathematics. We are now also offering visits to schools to run <a href="http://maths.org/paper-folding">workshops on the maths of paper folding</a>. <a href="http://mmp.maths.org/schoolvisits#NRICH-CPD">NRICH</a> staff also run problem-solving workshops and masterclasses for pupils, and professional development events for teachers.</p>
</div>
<p></p></article></div>
<div class="cam-column6">
<article class="cam-content-container cam-side-padding cam-sub-column-left-border cam-sub-column-right-border"><br /><a href="http://wild.maths.org"><img class="cam-scale-with-grid cam-float-left" src="/sites/mmp.maths.org/files/tt_iStock_Giraffes_X_crop-web.jpg" alt="Image: iStockphoto.com" /></a>
<div class="cam-content-container cam-vertical-teaser-txt">
<h3 class="cam-teaser-title"><a href="http://wild.maths.org">Wild Maths</a></h3>
<p>
Our new <a href="http://wild.maths.org">Wild Maths</a> resources are designed to encourage mathematical creativity. Mathematical activities, games, articles and videos invite you to explore, imagine, experiment and create. Developed by our NRICH and Plus teams, Wild Maths is aimed at ages 7 to 16, working individually or with a parent. </p>
</div>
<p></p></article></div>
</div>
<div class="cam-column6">
<article class="cam-content-container cam-side-padding cam-sub-column-left-border cam-sub-column-right-border"><br /><a href="http://sport.maths.org"><img class="cam-scale-with-grid cam-float-left" src="/sites/mmp.maths.org/files/tt_iStock_finishing%20line_0.jpg" alt="Image: iStockphoto.com" /></a>
<div class="cam-content-container cam-vertical-teaser-txt">
<h3 class="cam-teaser-title"><a href="http://sport.maths.org">Maths and Sport</a></h3>
<p>
To celebrate London 2012 we created free online resources exploring maths through the Olympic and Paralympic Games. <a href="http://sport.maths.org">'Maths and Sport: Countdown to the Games'</a> was a partner project in the London 2012 Get Set+ education programme. Resources include rich mathematical activities for Key Stages 1 to 5, articles, and video maths challenges produced with input from us by BBC Two Learning Zone. We also developed a small collection of activities on <a href="http://sport.maths.org/content/afc">Maths and Football</a> in collaboration with Arsenal in the Community.</p>
</div>
<p></p></article></div>
<hr class="cam-teaser-divider" /><div class="cam-column6">
<article class="cam-content-container cam-side-padding cam-sub-column-left-border cam-sub-column-right-border"><br /><img class="cam-scale-with-grid cam-float-left" src="/sites/mmp.maths.org/files/images/content/PrimeHopscotch.jpg" alt="" /><div class="cam-content-container cam-vertical-teaser-txt">
<h3 class="cam-teaser-title">Our local activities include:</h3>
<ul><li>hosting the <a href="http://www.furthermaths.org.uk/centre/index.php?centre=eastofengland" target="_blank">East of England Further Mathematics Support Programme</a> - teaching, support and promotion of maths beyond GCSE, especially Maths and Further Maths A-level </li>
<li><a href="/mmp-events">Mathematics events</a> in Cambridge throughout the year for schools, families and the general public, especially for the annual Cambridge Science Festival in March, and teacher professional development conferences </li>
<li>the <a href="http://www.mmp.maths.org/local">STIMULUS</a> programme, which creates over 250 placements each year for Cambridge student volunteers in local schools to assist with maths and science classes.</li>
</ul></div>
<p></p></article></div>
<hr class="cam-teaser-divider" /><div class="cam-column12">
<p>The MMP’s various programmes have won many awards and our resources have been repeatedly commended by the UK Government’s Department for Education. In 2006 the Queen presented the project with the Queen’s Anniversary Prize for Higher and Further Education, honouring ‘outstanding achievement and excellence’ at world-class leve<span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;">l. </span><span style="font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif;"> </span></p>
</div>
