                        <h2>A Conversation in Multiple Media on the Variability of Learners<br/></h2>
                        <p>Leading thinkers, researchers, and education practitioners discuss learning and human variability via video, audio, and the written word. Read more at <a href="http://udljournal.cast.org/">Conversations in Multiple Media</a>.<br/></p>
                    </div>
                </div>
                
                <div class="panel">
                    <div class="panel-wrapper">
                        <h2>Read a chapter from <em>Universal Design for Learning: Theory and Practice</em></h2>
                        
                        <p>Learners are highly variable, but that variation is not chaotic. Learners share common, predictable patterns of variability that are very useful to consider when designing educational environments. Read more from this new book by Anne Meyer, David H. Rose, and David Gordon: <a href="http://udltheorypractice.cast.org">udltheorypractice.cast.org</a></p>
                    </div>
                </div>
