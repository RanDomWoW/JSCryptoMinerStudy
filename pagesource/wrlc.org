<h1>Creating Synergies for Success</h1>
<p>The Washington Research Library Consortium (WRLC) was established as a non-profit corporation in 1987 to support and enhance the library and information services of universities in the Washington, DC metropolitan area. Currently, our partner universities are: </p>
<ul><li class="li3"><a href="au">American University</a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="cu">The Catholic University of America</a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="ga">Gallaudet University</a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="gm"><span class="s1">George Mason University</span></a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="gw">The George Washington University</a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="gt"><span class="s1">Georgetown University</span></a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="hu"><span class="s1">Howard University </span></a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="mu"><span class="s1">Marymount University</span></a></li>
<li class="li3"><a href="dc"><span class="s1">The University of the District of Columbia</span></a></li>
</ul><p class="li3"><span class="s1">The WRLC enables the success of learning and scholarship by creating coordinated collections, creating a robust infrastructure for discovery and access, ensuring the long-term preservation of physical and digital information resources and sharing expertise.</span></p>
