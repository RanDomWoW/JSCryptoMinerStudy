<p align="justify">SERSC is an international center for supporting distinguished 

  scholars and students who are researching various areas of Science and Technology.

  to discuss recent progress in various areas of Science and Technology.</p>

<p align="justify">SERSC organizes many international conferences, symposia and 

  workshops every year, and provides sponsor or technical support to researchers 

  who wish to organize their own conferences and workshops.<br>

  <br>

  areas of Science and Technology.</p>
