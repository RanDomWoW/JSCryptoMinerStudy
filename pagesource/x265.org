			<p>x265 is a H.265 / HEVC video encoder application library, designed to encode video or images into an H.265 / HEVC encoded bitstream.</p>
<h2>x265 Project Goals</h2>
<div id="attachment_283" style="max-width: 675px" class="wp-caption alignright"><a href="http://x265.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Figure-145-Ripping.png"><img class="wp-image-283 size-full" src="http://x265.org/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/675x489xFigure-145-Ripping-e1443133509288.png.pagespeed.ic.Y9YWH3G2qZ.jpg" alt="x265 best overall HEVC encoding efficiency" width="675" height="489"/></a><p class="wp-caption-text">x265 demonstrates best overall HEVC encoding efficiency in Moscow State University&#8217;s Codec Comparison</p></div>
<ul class="list">
<li>Develop the best HEVC (H.265) video encoder in the world<br/>
&#8211; Offer the highest possible quality at any given bit rate<br/>
&#8211; Offer the lowest possible bit rate for a given quality level</li>
<li>Deliver the world’s fastest and most computationally efficient HEVC encoder<br/>
&#8211; Highest encoding speed on any hardware configuration<br/>
&#8211; Highest visual quality, whether you are doing high quality offline encoding or high speed real-time encoding</li>
<li>Deliver industry-leading HEVC codec features, supporting the full HEVC standard including range extensions</li>
<li>Achieve wide adoption from the open source community, leading web video services, broadcast encoder developers, independent software developers, video hardware and software developers, device OEMs, semiconductor manufacturers and academics.</li>
<li>Provide the best possible support to developers, licensees and adopters</li>
</ul>
<h2>Try it</h2>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="https://x265.com">x265 Encoder application</a> combines a user-friendly interface with the x265 encoder library, making it easy for anyone to convert MP4 files to HEVC.</li>
