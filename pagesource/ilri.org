    <h2 class="ple" class="block-title">better lives through livestock</h2>
  
  <div class="plecontent" class="block-content content">
    <p>The International Livestock Research Institute (ILRI) works to improve food security and reduce poverty in developing countries through research for better and more sustainable use of livestock. ILRI is a <strong><a href="https://www.cgiar.org">CGIAR research centre</a></strong> – part of a global research partnership for a food-secure future.</p>
<p>See our <strong><a href="https://www.ilri.org/research">research programs and activities</a></strong> and our world class biotechnology research facilities <a href="https://www.ilri.org/becahub" target="_blank"><strong>BecA-ILRI Hub</strong></a> for East and Central Africa. See our work in <strong><a href="https://www.ilri.org/crp">CGIAR research programs</a></strong>; ILRI leads the <strong><a href="https://livestock.cgiar.org/" target="_blank">Livestock Research Program</a></strong>. In 2013 ILRI adopted a <strong><a href="https://www.ilri.org/mission">strategy</a></strong> covering 2013-2022.</p>
<p> </p>
