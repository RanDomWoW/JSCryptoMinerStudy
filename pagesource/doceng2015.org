                <div class="span9">
                    <p>DocEng is the leading international ACM Symposium for researchers,
                practitioners, developers, and users to explore cutting-edge ideas and to exchange
                techniques, tools, and experiences in the domain of document engineering.</p>
                    <p>DocEng is built on the tradition of <a target="_blank" href="http://www.documentengineering.org/">past symposia</a> previously held around the world including <a target="_blank" href="http://www.doceng2014.org/">Fort Collins</a> (2014), <a target="_blank" href="http://www.doceng2013.org/">Florence</a> (2013), <a target="_blank" href="http://doceng2012.wp.mines-telecom.fr/">Paris</a> (2012), and <a target="_blank" href="https://sites.google.com/site/doceng2011/">Mountain View</a> (2011).</p>
                    <p>DocEng 2015 will be held at <a target="_blank" href="http://www.epfl.ch">EPFL</a> in Lausanne, Switzerland, September 08-11, 2015.</p>
                    <p>The symposium emphasizes innovative approaches to document engineering
                technology, use of documents and document collections in real world applications,
                novel principles, tools and processes that improve our ability to create, manage,
                maintain, share, and productively use these.</p>
                    <p>DocEng is a single track conference attended by a community of academic and
                industrial researchers. It will be preceded by one day of workshops and
                tutorials.</p>
                    <p>Students presenting their work at DocEng 2015 will be eligible to apply for
                SIGWEB Student Travel Awards.</p>
