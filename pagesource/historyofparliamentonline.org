      <h2 class="title">Using the History of Parliament</h2>
  
  <div class="content">
    <div id="using-hop">
Welcome to the History of Parliament online. This site contains all of the biographical, constituency and introductory survey articles published in The History of Parliament series. Work is still underway on checking and cleaning the data that has been transferred into the website from a number of sources, and the current version of the site is still provisional. In order to find out more about the articles produced by the History, click on the links in the 'Research' section above. Additional material - explanatory articles, and images of Members, Parliaments and elections - have been produced specially for the website, and can be found through the 'Explore' and 'Gallery' sections above. For more information on the History, see the <a href="/about">About us section</a>, follow us on <a href="http://www.facebook.com/HistoryOfParliament" target="_blank">Facebook</a> and <a href="https://twitter.com/HistParl" target="_blank">Twitter</a> or read the <a href="http://thehistoryofparliament.wordpress.com" target="_blank">HistoryOfParliament</a>, <a href="http://historyofparliamentblog.wordpress.com" target="_blank">Director</a> and <a href="http://victoriancommons.wordpress.com" target="_blank">VictorianCommons</a> blogs.</div>  </div>
