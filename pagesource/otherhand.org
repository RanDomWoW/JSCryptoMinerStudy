<p><span style="font-size: medium;"><strong><span style="color: #000000;">If you&#8217;re here, you must be seriously lost. I&#8217;m sorry for your misfortune.</span></strong></span></p>
<p>That said, if you&#8217;d like to hang around a while and see what&#8217;s here, then feel free. But be warned, I&#8217;m pretty damn good at wasting other people&#8217;s time. <p>OK, moving on, what you&#8217;ll find on this site is a chronicle of my various (mis)adventures going all the way back to <a href="http://www.otherhand.org/home-page/area-51-and-other-strange-places/bluefire-main/">my old Bluefire website</a> from the latter part of the last century. There&#8217;s no advertising on this site nor is it monetized in any way. It&#8217;s just something I felt like doing. Next time I&#8217;ll consider stamp collecting instead.</p>
