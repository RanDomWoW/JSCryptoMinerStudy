<h1>Barcoding  Biodiversity in the Philippines</h1>
<p>DNALC Executive Director Dave Micklos recently spent a week introducing  teachers from the Philippine Science High School System to methods for lab  instruction in molecular genetics and genomics. 
<p>The workshop was held in  the new science lab on the Eastern Vismayas Campus developed as part of the  2016 Breakthrough Junior Challenge  award for Popular Vote winner, <a href="https://youtu.be/jdStp0Xk_qw">Hillary Diane Andales</a>. Dave took the educators on a field trip to conduct  a mini ecological study using DNA barcoding to identify plants, animals, and  fungi from the local habitat.
<h3 style="margin-bottom: 5px;">Another win for Hillary!</h3>
<p>During the December 3, 2017 award ceremony broadcast, we were excited to learn   that Hillary is  the 2017 Breakthrough Junior Challenge winner! Visit the <a href="https://breakthroughjuniorchallenge.org/">Breakthrough Junior Challenge</a> site to see her winning video. <a href="/about/international/breakthrough.html">Learn more about the DNA Learning Center's involvement with the Breakthrough Junior Challenge.</a></p>
