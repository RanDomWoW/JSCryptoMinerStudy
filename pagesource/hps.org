<h1>Welcome to the Health Physics Society</h1>

<p>Find information and answers to your questions about radiation and radiation safety/protection. For many years radiation has been beneficial to human beings for medical diagnosis and therapy, scientific research, and generating electrical power. However, when used in unsafe ways, radiation can harm people. Care must be taken to properly use radiation and to minimize unnecessary radiation exposures. The health physicist's job is to manage the beneficial use of radiation. <span style="float:right;"><a href="/aboutthesociety/">Learn more</a></span></p>
