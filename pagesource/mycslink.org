  <div class="content--lg">
    <div class="accordion-set grey-box">
      <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
      <ul class="accordion">
  <li>
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">How do I sign up for My CS-Link?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          The next time you visit your physician's office:
        </p>
        <ul class="list list--bulleted">
          <li>Ask for an activation letter, which will include a one-time-use access code for your account</li>
          <li>Visit <a href="http://mycslink.org/">mycslink.org</a> </li>
          <li>Click on "New User Signup"</li>
          <li>Select "I have an access code"</li>
          <li>Fill in the information</li>
        </ul>
        <p>
          You can also get your own access code:
        </p>
        <ul class="list list--bulleted">
          <li>Visit <a href="http://mycslink.org/">mycslink.org</a> </li>
          <li>Click on "I need access for myself"</li>
          <li>Complete the My CS-Link sign-up form; this will give you an access code</li>
          <li>Go to "I have an access code"</li>
        </ul>
        <p>
          The information you provide is private and is processed through a secure website.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">What is My CS-Link<sup>&trade;</sup>?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        My CS-Link is a secure online health management tool that connects you to your personal health information.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Who is eligible to join?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        If you are a Cedars-Sinai patient and your doctor uses My CS-Link, you are eligible for a My CS-Link account.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Where do I get help or more information?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          If you have questions about My CS-Link, please call Patient Services at 800-700-6424. For technical support, call 855-427-5465. For medical emergencies, call 911.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" id="connectdevices" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">What devices and apps sync with My CS-Link?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          The Apple Health app and Apple HealthKit work with multiple devices that track weight, steps, heart rate, blood oxygen levels, blood pressure and blood sugar, including:
        </p>
        <ul class="list list--bulleted">
          <li>Apple Watch</li>
          <li>Fitbit devices including:
            <ul class="list list--bulleted list__sublist list__sublist--circle-marker">
              <li>Activity trackers</li>
              <li>Aria Smart Scale</li>
            </ul>
          </li>
          <li>Nike FuelBand</li>
          <li>Withings devices including:
            <ul class="list list--bulleted list__sublist list__sublist--circle-marker">
              <li>Smart Body Analyzer</li>
              <li>Pulse O2</li>
              <li>Blood Pressure Monitor</li>
            </ul>
          </li>
        </ul>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>




  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">How do I connect my device?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <ul class="list list--bulleted">
          <li>Set up your wearable device</li>
          <li>Log into your My CS-Link account</li>
          <li>Go to "My Medical Record"</li>
          <li>Select "Track My Health" </li>
          <li>Click "Manage Devices" </li>
          <li>Connect your wearable devices on the "Health Tracker Accounts" page</li>
          <li>Sync your data or add it manually</li>
          <li>View what you've tracked in the logs</li>
        </ul>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">How do I connect My CS-Link mobile application with Apple's Health app on my iPhone or iPod touch?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <ul class="list list--bulleted">
          <li>Log into the My CS-Link app</li>
          <li>Select "Track My Health" icon</li>
          <li>Select "Connect to Health"</li>
          <li>Choose the types of data that you want to sync with My CS-Link</li>
        </ul>
        <p>
          If you have a Withings device, you need to ensure your profile is set to share with Apple's Health app. Then follow the steps above, and the Health app will serve as a bridge to automatically load data from Withings to My CS-Link.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">How do I sync my data?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          If you have Apple and Withings devices, data will auto-sync with My CS-Link through Apple HealthKit. If you have an Android or Windows device, you can manually sync your Withings device data by logging into My CS-Link and clicking the "Sync Now" button.
        </p>
        <p>
          <strong>Note: Your physician's office will have access to information uploaded to My CS-Link from your personal health devices, but will not routinely monitor it. Please contact your physician if you would like to discuss information from one of your connected devices.</strong>
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">How can My CS-Link help me manage and understand my healthcare?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        It brings together key parts of your medical information. With My CS-Link you can contact your physician's office, request and schedule appointments at your convenience, and receive electronic health information and reminders.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Do I need any special computer equipment to use My CS-Link?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        No. All you need is access to a computer or smart mobile device, an email account and an internet connection.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">What is OpenNotes?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        OpenNotes is a new tool offered via <a href="https://www.mycslink.org/">My CS-Link™</a> that allows patients to view their doctor’s detailed notes after each office visit.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Where can I view my doctor's progress note?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        You can access your doctor’s notes by taking the following steps:
        <ol class="list list--numbered">
          <li>Log on to <a href="http://mycslink.org/">My CS-Link</a></li>
          <li>Click on "Appointments" tab</li>
          <li>Select "Past Appts"</li>
          <li>Select the appointment date</li>
          <li>Click on "Notes" tab</li>
        </ol>
        At this time, OpenNotes is not available via mobile app.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">I recently visited my doctor’s office — why can’t I see my progress note?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        Your notes will NOT be viewable on My CS-Link if
        <ul class="list list--bulleted">
          <li>Your doctor is not participating in this program</li>
          <li>Your doctor hasn’t closed the appointment</li>
          <li>Your doctor chose to hide the note</li>
          <li>You had an inpatient or unscheduled visit (e.g., telephone)</li>
          <li>Your notes were marked as sensitive; currently this applies to notes surrounding transplants</li>
          <li>Your notes were written by a doctor whose <strong>specialty</strong> is <strong>psychiatry</strong> or <strong>psychology</strong></li>
          <li>Your notes were written within an outpatient <strong>department</strong> that specializes in <strong>psychiatry</strong> or <strong>psychology</strong></li>
          <li>You are younger than 18</li>
          <li>You are in the CS-Link mobile app</li>
        </ul>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Will I be able to see my doctor's historical notes?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        No. With the exception of patients who were part of the pilot program, notes prior to Nov. 6, 2016, will not be viewable on My CS-Link.
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Can I email my primary care physician through My CS-Link?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          Yes. If your physician uses My CS-Link, you can contact your doctor using your account. You will be notified via email whenever new information, such as a reply from your doctor, is available. Information from your healthcare team will be located in secure messages that only you can see by logging into your My CS-Link account.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Will my physician contact me through My CS-Link?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          Yes. If your doctor uses My CS-Link, you may get secure messages about select test results and other health-related matters through your account.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Does every Cedars-Sinai patient automatically have My CS-Link?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          No. An account can be created only at your request. Cedars-Sinai does not require anyone to sign up for My CS-Link.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">Is My CS-Link health information secure?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          We take great care to ensure your health information is kept private and secure. Access to information is controlled through secure access codes, personal IDs and personal passwords. My CS-Link uses the latest technology to automatically encrypt your session, and all messaging takes place while you are securely logged on to our website.
        </p>
      </li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li style="display:none;">
    <a id="proxy-access" href="javascript:void(0)" class="js-accordion-trigger faq-toggle">How do I get proxy access for another person?</a>
    <ul class="submenu answers">
      <li>
        <p>
          To help patients and their care partners manage their healthcare needs, proxy access allows a patient's designated representative ("proxy") to have access to the patient's medical information through My CS-Link. If you are a parent or care partner of a Cedars-Sinai patient and you would like to gain access to the patient’s medical information via My CS-Link, please complete one of the forms below and take it to the patient’s next physician office visit.
        </p>

        <ul class="list faq__list">
          <li>
            <a href="https://www.cedars-sinai.edu/Patients/Programs-and-Services/My-CS-Link/Child-under-12-Proxy-Request-Form-1214.pdf">Proxy Access for Children Under Age 12 (PDF)</a>
