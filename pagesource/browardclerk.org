                                             Effective April 2, 2018, the <a href="https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/News/passports/execution-fee-increases.html" target="_blank">U.S. Department of State</a> is increasing the passport execution fee from $25 to $35. The $10 execution fee increase only applies to U.S. passport applicants using the DS-11 form, such as first-time applicants over 16, children under 16, and applicants who re-apply after reporting their previous passport lost or stolen.
                                         </p>
