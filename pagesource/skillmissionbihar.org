		<h2 itemprop="name">
							Welcome to Bihar Skill Development Mission Portal 									</h2>
				<p class="subtitle">Empowering the Youth</p>
									</div>
					
	
	
		
								<div itemprop="articleBody">
		<p><span>Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM) has been constituted by The Government of Bihar to empower the youth by providing them with requisite skills to fuel the growth of the State of Bihar. </span></p>
<p><span>The primary roles of BSDM are: </span></p>
<ul>
<li>To establish a wide network of training centers for the youth</li>
<li>To provide employment opportunities to the youth</li>
</ul>
<p><span> </span></p>
<div class="row services">
<div class="col-md-6">
<div class="service-item"><i class="fa fa-graduation-cap"></i>
<h4>Courses</h4>
<p>Interested trainees can visit their nearest training center to select a course of their choice</p>
</div>
<div class="service-item"><i class="fa fa-star-o"></i>
<h4>Certification</h4>
<p>Trainees who clear the assessment successfully will be certified</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="col-md-6">
<div class="service-item"><i class="fa fa-book"></i>
<h4>Assessment</h4>
<p>Trainees will be thoroughly assessed through collaborative mechanisms</p>
</div>
<div class="service-item"><i class="fa fa-globe"></i>
<h4>Employment</h4>
<p>Certified trainees will be provided with employment opportunities</p>
</div>
</div>
</div> 	</div>
