						<p>We are happy to announce this year's issue of the Linux Audio Conference<br/>
						(LAC) to take place at <a href="https://c-base.org" title="c-base" target="_blank">c-base</a>, Berlin, Germany during <b>April 8-10</b><br/>

						<p>The <a href="https://c-base.org" title="c-base" target="_blank">space station below Berlin-Mitte</a> and members of <a href="http://linuxaudio.berlin" title="Linux Audio Users Berlin" target="_blank">Linux Audio Users Berlin</a><br/>
						welcome all attendees, interested in the field of pro-audio on Linux<br/>
						to join in a weekend of lectures, workshops, hacking and getting together.<br/>
						</p>
