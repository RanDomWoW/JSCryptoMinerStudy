<p class="justify"><span class="gnucash">GnuCash</span> is personal and small-business financial-accounting software, freely licensed under the <a href="http://www.gnu.org/">GNU</a> GPL and available for GNU/Linux, BSD, Solaris, Mac OS X and Microsoft Windows.</p>

<p class="justify">Designed to be easy to use, yet powerful and flexible, <span class="gnucash">GnuCash</span> allows you to track bank accounts, stocks, income and expenses.  As quick and intuitive to use as a checkbook register, it is based on professional accounting principles to ensure balanced books and accurate reports. </p>
