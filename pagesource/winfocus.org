	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p>The <strong>World Interactive Network Focused On Critical UltraSound</strong> (WINFOCUS) is the world leader scientific network committed to develop <em>Point-of-Care Ultrasound</em> practice, research, education, technology, and networking, addressing the needs of patients, institutions, services, and communities living in out-of-hospital and in-hospital critical scenarios. Our <strong>goals</strong>:</p>

		</div>
	</div>
<div class="vc_row wpb_row vc_inner vc_row-fluid"><div class="wpb_column vc_column_container vc_col-sm-6"><div class="vc_column-inner "><div class="wpb_wrapper">
	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<ul>
<li>To identify <strong>critical scenarios</strong> where ultrasound, when implemented and incorporated into point-of-care, represents a critical added value to improve management, performance and outcomes;</li>
<li>To identify <strong>ultrasound “point-of-care” applications</strong> within <strong>extra-hospital</strong> and <strong>in-hospital scenarios</strong>, consistent with prevention, triage, diagnosis, monitoring and treatment of the critical patient;</li>
<li>To identify the <strong>technical, clinical, organizational, and social competencies</strong> necessary for effective incorporation and diffusion of “point-of-care” ultrasound into critical scenarios, the <strong>methodology, technology, and instrumentation</strong> necessary for its implementation, and the <strong>feasibility, sustainability, and accuracy</strong> of the identified technologies;</li>
<li>To evaluate and compare the experiences of different investigators through <strong>networking actions</strong>;</li>
<li>To develop <strong>research projects and conferences</strong>;</li>
<li>To promote <strong>quality assured training programs</strong>, incorporating them, where appropriate, into school and university curricula.</li>
</ul>
