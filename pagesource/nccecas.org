<p>
<strong>02/15/2018 - Issue Resolved for Impacted CECAS Users</strong>
</p>
<p>The issue reported earlier this morning impacting some users has been resolved.  We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.  If you have any questions regarding this or any other CECAS issue, please contact your Regional Trainer. If you cannot reach your Regional Trainer, contact the Help Desk at (919) 807-4357, press 3, then 1 for CECAS.
			</p>
<p>
<strong>02/15/2018 - CECAS ALERT - Server Restart Scheduled</strong>
</p>
<p>In order to try and correct the issue that we are experiencing with CECAS we are going to perform a RESTART of the servers today starting at 11:00 a.m. February 15, 2018.  Please be sure and save all information before 10:50 a.m. and log out of CECAS.  Do not log back in until you receive another message from us that all servers are back up and running.  You may also check the Comm site for updates. 
			</p>
<p>
<strong>01/12/2018 - December 2017 Child Count Change Report</strong>
</p>
<p>The 2017 <a href="http://www.nccecas.org/downloads/downloads/2017%20Year%20to%20Year%20Change%20Report.xls">Year to Year Change Report</a> showing the student count changes for each LEA from the December 2016 Child Count to the December 2017 Child Count has been posted to the CECAS information site on the documents tab under the Periodic Count section. Please look at the report and if your LEA, Charter school or State Operated Program has been highlighted in red, it has shown a significant increase or decrease of more than 20% and more than 20 students, the EC Director should email an explanation for the change to <a href="mailto:kelley.blas@dpi.nc.gov">kelley.blas@dpi.nc.gov</a> by 2/1/2018. We appreciate your timely response to this request.
			</p>
<p>

<strong>10/09/2017 - 2017 Data Submission for Indicator 12</strong>
</p>
<p>Indicator 12 will be submitted on spreadsheets by all LEAs. Charter Schools do not need to submit an Indicator 12 spreadsheet this year. The Indicator 12 spreadsheet has a tab with helpful clarifications to assist LEAs in entering their data. The spreadsheet is posted on the CECAS communication site on the <a href="http://www.nccecas.org/downloads/downloads.html">Documents tab</a> under Periodic Count.
			 </p>
<p>The due dates for the Indicator 11 and 12 are listed below. Your cooperation in meeting the deadlines for data submission is greatly appreciated. Please email all Indicator spreadsheets to <a href="mailto:ecindicators@dpi.nc.gov">ecindicators@dpi.nc.gov</a>
			
</p>
<p>Indicator 11 - October 20, 2017<br>
				Indicator 12 - October 20, 2017
			</p>
<p>
<strong>10/02/2017 - Indicator 11 Count Opens Today</strong>
</p>
<p>Indicator 11 is now open for submissions. All test counts have been deleted. Please submit an initial submission by October 13, 2017. <strong>All LEAs, Charter schools, and State Operated Programs that completed a December 2016 child count</strong> must submit and certify Indicator 11 no later than <strong>October 20, 2017.</strong> 
			
</p>
<p>
<strong>Submitting the Count: </strong>
<br>
				The EC Director must sign into CECAS.<br>
				Open Data Exchange folder, Open the Periodic Count folder, Click on Periodic Count Submissions.<br>
				On the Periodic Count Submissions page, click New Submission.  Fill in the drop lists on the screen.<br>
				Click Next.
			</p>
<p>
<strong>To Certify the Count: </strong>
<br>
				Make sure the count status is either NO DATA or VALIDATION COMPLETED<br>
				The EC Director must sign into CECAS. Open Data Exchange folder, Open the Periodic Count folder, Click on Periodic Count Submissions.<br>
				On the Periodic Count Submissions page, drop down the Count Period Type field.  From the Count Period Type drop list select SY 2016-17 Indicator 11 Count.<br>
				Select Region and LEA.  <strong>Do not select a FILE TYPE.</strong>
<br>
				Click Search. The Certify button will appear next to the Search button.<br>
				Click Certify.<br>
			
</p>
<p>Print the next page for your records.
			</p>
<p>If you have any questions regarding this, please contact your Regional Trainer. If you cannot reach your Regional Trainer, contact the Help Desk at (919) 807-4357, press 3, then 1 for CECAS.
			</p>

<p>Indicator 7 will be submitted using the CECAS system this year. No changes have been made to this process for the 16-17 end of year collection.
