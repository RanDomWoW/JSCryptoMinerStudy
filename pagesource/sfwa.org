	<h2 class="post_title"><a href="http://www.sfwa.org/2018/03/building-worlds/" title="Building Worlds">Building Worlds</a></h2>	<div class="contents_wrapper">
					<div class="body">
					<p>by K. C. Norton</p>
<p>For the past three years, I have been working as a ghostwriter, writing coach, and English tutor. In my line of work, I almost invariably partner with people who are writing or editing their memoirs. For the most part, they’re content to know that I have a Master’s degree, but occasionally they want to know if I’ve published anything, and if so, what kind of stories I write for myself. I both dread and relish this moment, because it’s a chance to reveal my dirty secret: my preferred genre is Science Fiction.</p>
