      <p class="hidden" "popupclose">SCHOOLS ARE  FEBRUARY 21!<br>
        It will, however, be quite cold, so we are taking the following steps to keep our students as safe as possible as they travel to school in the morning:<br>
- As we do on any extremely cold days, our transportation team will begin warming our buses up at least an hour early.<br>
- All schools will open their doors 30 minutes early.
