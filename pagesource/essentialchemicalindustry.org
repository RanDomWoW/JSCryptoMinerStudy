<h1 style="text-align: left;"><span style="line-height: 1.3em;">Welcome to The Essential Chemical Industry - online</span></h1>
<p style="text-align: left;">This is a reference library of the worldâ€™s principal industrial chemicals, their uses and their manufacture using current industrial processes and innovations. &nbsp;This new web-based edition was first developed in 2013 from the book of the same title, which went through five editions. &nbsp;It is intended for:</p>
<ul style="text-align: left;">
<li>school, college and university students and their teachers</li>
<li>industrial chemists and employees of chemical companies needing a quick reference or overview</li>
</ul>
<h4><span style="font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 1em; font-weight: bold; line-height: 1.3em;">About the The Essential Chemical Industry - online</span></h4>
<p>Like the book was,&nbsp;<em><strong>The Essential Chemical Industry (ECI</strong>) <strong>- online</strong>&nbsp;</em>is now one of the most highly respected and up-to-date resources about the chemical industry. &nbsp;This innovative website has easy cross-referencing and drill-down capability and achieves a new level of access for the student, researcher and industrial chemist alike. &nbsp;It has an exciting interactive research capability.</p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="line-height: 1.3em;">It is produced by </span><strong><a href="http://www.ciec.org.uk">Centre for Industry Education Collaboration (CIEC)</a></strong><span style="line-height: 1.3em;">&nbsp;formerly the Chemical Industry Education Centre, an independent non-profit organization in the <strong><a href="http://www.york.ac.uk/chemistry/">Department of Chemistry</a></strong>, University of York, UK.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Written by members of the Centreâ€™s staff, and with the advice of over 200 experts in the field, the site is systematically reviewed and updated to ensure that it remains relevant and authoritative.&nbsp;</p>
<h4><strong>How to use this site</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: left;">You can simply dive in and enjoy a journey of exploration - OR you can research information with more precision. &nbsp;Follow the <a href="/?start=1"><strong>link</strong></a> for some useful tips on finding your way around the site.</p>
