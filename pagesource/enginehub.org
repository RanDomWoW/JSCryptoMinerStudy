<h1 class="landing-header">Open-source mods for and by the Minecraft community</h1>
<p class="text-center landing-text">
We're a collection of mods, plugins, and tools created for Minecraft by members of the community. Our
projects are all open source and power all varieties of servers &mdash; from the large to
the family-sized. Many of our projects are available, officially or
unofficially, for Bukkit, Minecraft Forge, MinecraftEdu, LiteLoader, CanaryMod,
and other platforms.
