				<h3><a href="/articles/dirham">DIRHAM</a></h3> 
				<p>a unit of silver coinage and of weight. The dirham retained a stable value of about 4 g throughout the entire pre-Islamic period. The tetradrachm, or stater (&gt; Pahl. <em>stēr</em>), was equivalent to 4 drachmas and was already in circulation in the Achaemenid period at the time of Alexander&rsquo;s departure for Persia.</p>
