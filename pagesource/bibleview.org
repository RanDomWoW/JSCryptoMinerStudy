<h3>A timeline of the Bible illustrated with over 800 pages and over 600 Images or pictures</h3>

<p>The idea that a Historical Chronological Overview of the Bible could be used as a vehicle to explain the Bible started over thirty years ago, as we found that many people did not know the contents of their Bible, let alone how the Bible fitted together. It is only in the last ten years that we have tried to put what still is a successful live presentation system and transport it to the web. The design of the website follows this tradition.</p>
<p>Supplementary information such as teaching notes, resources and background information has been added to some of the English modules. A number of files of information are kept off line. Some of these can be emailed on request. A list of these files is being prepared.</p>
<p>The three most important Links are: <a href="/en/bible/"><span class="link">Old Testament</span></a>, <a href="/en/bible/#anchor"><span class="link">New Testament</span></a> and <a href="/en/timelines/undated -past-1800bc/"><span class="link">Timelines</span></a><br />
