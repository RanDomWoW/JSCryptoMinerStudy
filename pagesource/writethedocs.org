<h1>Welcome to our community!<a class="headerlink" href="#welcome-to-our-community" title="Permalink to this headline">¶</a></h1>
<p><strong>Write the Docs</strong> is a global community of people who care about documentation. Our primary gathering places are:</p>
<ul class="simple">
<li><a class="reference internal" href="slack/"><span class="doc">Our slack network</span></a> with thousands of members</li>
<li><a class="reference internal" href="conf/"><span class="doc">Conferences</span></a> on 3 continents</li>
<li><a class="reference internal" href="meetups/"><span class="doc">Local meetups</span></a> in over 30 cities</li>
</ul>
<p>We consider everyone who cares about communication, documentation, and
their users to be a member of our community. This can be programmers,
tech writers, developer advocates, customer support, marketers, and anyone else who wants
people to have great experiences with software.</p>
<p>Our conferences create a time and a place for the global community of
<a class="reference internal" href="documentarians/"><span class="doc">Documentarians</span></a> to share information, discuss ideas, and work together
to improve the <strong>art and science of documentation</strong>.</p>
