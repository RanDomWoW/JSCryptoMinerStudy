      <h2 class="news_title">5th WorldCALL Conference Chile</h2>
      <span class="news_category">お知らせ</span>　<span class="news_post">本部</span><br>
      <br>
      第5回WorldCALL (2018年11月)のご案内が実行委員長から届いています:<br />
期日:2018年11月13〜16日<br />
会場:the Universidad de Concepción, Chile<br />
大会サイト:<a href="http://worldcall5.org">http://worldcall5.org</a><br />
発表受付締切:2018年1月31日<br />
<br />
以下、Emerita Bañados-Santana実行委員長からのご挨拶です:<br />
=====<br />
5th WorldCALL Conference Chile<br />
November 13th to 16th, 2018<br />
CALLing all the CALLers Worldwide<br />
   We are pleased to invite you to submit a proposal for the 5th WorldCALL Conference, to be held in Concepción, CHILE, from 13 to 16 November 2018, hosted by the Universidad de Concepción. The 5th WorldCALL Conference welcomes contributions from researchers, teachers, practitioners, technology experts and professionals working in Computer Assisted Language Learning that concern rationale, theories, research, pedagogies and innovative practices using technology for first/second/foreign language teaching and learning.<br />
<br />
   As an international association, WorldCALL provides a worldwide platform for the promulgation of innovative research, development and practice in education and training; enhances the opportunities for knowledge and skills transfer to nations currently underserved in the area of CALL; and serves as a forum for exchanging information and forging professional relationships among educators, researchers and industry leaders from around the world. WorldCALL has held an international conference every five years since its first conference in Australia in 1998. Since then, conferences have been held in Canada in 2003, in Japan in 2008 and in the UK in 2013.<br />
<br />
   We invite proposals for paper presentations, pre-conference workshops, symposia, poster sessions, technology showcases, and vendor exhibitions.  For more information about the call for proposal submissions, please click here. For further information about the 5th WoldCALL Conference, please visit our website worldcall5.org.<br />
<br />
   We look forward to seeing you in Concepción, Chile, in this 5th gathering of the WorldCALL International Association, November 13-16, 2018; so note these dates on your calendar and make arrangements to attend.<br />
<br />
Emerita Bañados-Santana<br />
Chair, Local Organising Committee<br />
WorldCALL 2018<br />
