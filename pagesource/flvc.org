provide statewide innovative educational services for Florida's K-adult students. Working collaboratively with Florida’s 12 public universities, 28 public colleges, K-12 school districts, and other partners, FLVC provides free services to help students go to college, succeed in school, prepare for career success, and in life after graduation.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong>The purpose of FLVC is to:</strong></span></p> <ul> <li>Provide information regarding and access to distance learning courses and degree programs offered by public postsecondary education institutions within&nbsp;Florida.</li> <li>Coordinate with the Florida College System and the State University System to identify and provide online academic support services and resources when the multi-institutional provision of such services and resources is more cost effective or operationally effective.</li> <li>Administer the Florida Academic Library Services Cooperative established in s. 1006.73 and consult with the chancellors of the Florida College System and the State University System regarding the implementation and operations of the cooperative.</li> <li>Facilitate degree completion for the state’s adult learners through the Complete Florida Degree Initiative.</li> </ul> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p><span style="font-size: 16px;"><strong>Units&nbsp;under the Florida Virtual Campus include:</strong></span></p> <ul> <li><a href="https://libraries.flvc.org/">The Florida Academic Library Services Cooperative</a></li> <li><a href="https://dlss.flvc.org/">Distance Learning and Student Services</a></li> </ul> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p>The&nbsp;Florida Virtual Campus and the Complete Florida Plus Program operate <a href="https://www.floridashines.org">FloridaShines</a>, a statewide hub for delivering innovative educational services that operate collaboratively across Florida’s public education institutions and educational systems.&nbsp; The Complete Florida Plus Program also includes the <a href="https://www.completeflorida.org">Complete Florida Degree Initiative</a> and the <a href="https://www.floridashines.org/find-a-career">MyCareerShines</a> career planning and preparation service.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p>The Complete Florida Plus Program is established in F.S. 1006.735.&nbsp; The Florida Virtual Campus is funded by the Florida Legislature and administered by the
