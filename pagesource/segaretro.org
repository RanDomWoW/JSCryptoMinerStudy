<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-bodytext">
<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>Welcome to <b>Sega Retro</b>, a project from the people behind <a href="http://info.sonicretro.org/Sonic_Retro" class="extiw" title="sonic:Sonic Retro">Sonic Retro</a>. We aim to cover everything possible about Sega from the 1940s to today. We currently are working on <a href="/Special:Statistics" title="Special:Statistics">15,033</a> <a href="/Special:AllPages" title="Special:AllPages">articles</a> and have many more that are <a href="/Special:WantedPages" title="Special:WantedPages">requested</a> to be worked on.
</p><p>While this wiki is designed to help the community by providing information, it is also dependent on the community to be updated. Anyone with an account may edit the wiki. As you feel more comfortable with the site, please feel free to make any changes or additions that you feel necessary.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Get_involved.21">Get involved!</span></h3>
<p>You can become a part of the Sega Retro community in several different ways:
</p>
<ul><li> Sign up for a Sega Retro account and become part of our group of editors <i>(If you have a Sonic Retro account please use the same username for your account here)</i></li>
<li> We have lots of <a href="/Category:Stubs" title="Category:Stubs">game</a> <a href="/Category:Sub-stubs" title="Category:Sub-stubs">pages</a> that are in desperate need of more information. If you know your way around some of these games, help us out by telling us how to play or what to expect. We also have <a href="/Special:WantedPages" title="Special:WantedPages">many pages that still need to be made</a>.</li>
<li> We are always on the lookout for <a href="/Sega_Retro:Scanning" class="mw-redirect" title="Sega Retro:Scanning">high quality, unmarked, genuine</a> game media scans. If you notice a poor quality or missing scan of a game you have, please help by making a scan and replacing the bad one. You can use our <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://andlabs.sonicretro.org/scans/">scan tracker</a> to track our progress.</li>
<li> Log on to <a href="http://info.sonicretro.org/BadnikNET" class="extiw" title="sonic:BadnikNET">BadnikNET</a> (<a rel="nofollow" class="external free" href="irc://irc.badnik.net">irc://irc.badnik.net</a>) and join #sega, our dedicated IRC channel</li>
<li> From another country or speak another language? Help us by providing otherwise difficult-to-get information about Sega in mainland Europe, Asia, Africa, and South America, or by translating texts from <a href="/Category:Articles_needing_translation_from_Japanese" title="Category:Articles needing translation from Japanese">Japanese</a>.</li>
<li> Short on ideas? <a href="/Sega_Retro:Todo" title="Sega Retro:Todo">Visit the "to do" page</a></li></ul>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="On_this_day.2C_March_18...">On this day, March 18...</span></h3>
<p>March 18 (<span class="plainlinks"><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://segaretro.org/index.php?title=Sega_Retro:Anniversaries/March_18&amp;action=edit">add</a></span>):<br />
<a href="/File:SegaWorldSydney.png" class="image"><img alt="SegaWorldSydney.png" src="/images/thumb/5/5b/SegaWorldSydney.png/400px-SegaWorldSydney.png" width="400" height="264" srcset="/images/thumb/5/5b/SegaWorldSydney.png/600px-SegaWorldSydney.png 1.5x, /images/thumb/5/5b/SegaWorldSydney.png/800px-SegaWorldSydney.png 2x" /></a>
</p>
<ul><li><b>1994</b> - In Japan, <i><a href="/Bare_Knuckle_III" class="mw-redirect" title="Bare Knuckle III">Bare Knuckle III</a></i>, <i><a href="/Vampire_Killer" class="mw-redirect" title="Vampire Killer">Vampire Killer</a></i> and <i><a href="/Virtua_Racing" title="Virtua Racing">Virtua Racing</a></i> are released for the <a href="/Mega_Drive" class="mw-redirect" title="Mega Drive">Mega Drive</a>, <i><a href="/F1_Circus_CD" title="F1 Circus CD">F1 Circus CD</a></i>, <i><a href="/Game_no_Kanzume_Vol.1" title="Game no Kanzume Vol.1">Game no Kanzume Vol.1</a></i>, <i><a href="/Game_no_Kanzume_Vol.2" title="Game no Kanzume Vol.2">Game no Kanzume Vol.2</a></i> and <i><a href="/Heimdall" title="Heimdall">Heimdall</a></i> arrive on the <a href="/Mega-CD" class="mw-redirect" title="Mega-CD">Mega-CD</a> and <i><a href="/Sonic_Drift" title="Sonic Drift">Sonic Drift</a></i> and <i><a href="/Yuu_Yuu_Hakusho:_Horobishi_Mono_no_Gyakushuu" title="Yuu Yuu Hakusho: Horobishi Mono no Gyakushuu">Yuu Yuu Hakusho: Horobishi Mono no Gyakushuu</a></i> debut on the <a href="/Game_Gear" class="mw-redirect" title="Game Gear">Game Gear</a>.</li>
<li><b>1997</b> - <a href="/Sega_World_Sydney" title="Sega World Sydney">Sega World Sydney</a> opens to the general public.</li>
<li><b>1998</b> - the album, <i><a href="/Harley-Davidson_%26_L.A._Riders_(album)" title="Harley-Davidson &amp; L.A. Riders (album)">Harley-Davidson &amp; L.A. Riders</a></i> is released in Japan.</li>
<li><b>1999</b> - <i><a href="/Idol_Janshi_Suchie-Pai_Secret_Album" title="Idol Janshi Suchie-Pai Secret Album">Idol Janshi Suchie-Pai Secret Album</a></i> and <i><a href="/Kiss_Yori..." title="Kiss Yori...">Kiss Yori...</a></i> are released for the <a href="/Sega_Saturn" title="Sega Saturn">Sega Saturn</a> in Japan. <i><a href="/Kita_e._White_Illumination" title="Kita e. White Illumination">Kita e. White Illumination</a></i> is released for Japanese <a href="/Dreamcast" class="mw-redirect" title="Dreamcast">Dreamcasts</a> and <i><a href="/Waku_Puyo_Dungeon_Ketteiban" title="Waku Puyo Dungeon Ketteiban">Waku Puyo Dungeon Ketteiban</a></i> is released for the Japanese PlayStation.</li>
<li><b>2002</b> - <i><a href="/Gunvalkyrie" title="Gunvalkyrie">Gunvalkyrie</a></i> is released in North America for the <a href="/Xbox" title="Xbox">Xbox</a>, alongside the <a href="/Nintendo_GameCube" title="Nintendo GameCube">Nintendo GameCube</a> games <i><a href="/Home_Run_King" title="Home Run King">Home Run King</a></i> and <i><a href="/Sega_Soccer_Slam" title="Sega Soccer Slam">Sega Soccer Slam</a></i>.</li>
<li><b>2003</b> - <i><a href="/Sonic_Advance_2" title="Sonic Advance 2">Sonic Advance 2</a></i> is released for the <a href="/Game_Boy_Advance" title="Game Boy Advance">Game Boy Advance</a> in Europe. <i><a href="/The_King_of_Route_66" title="The King of Route 66">The King of Route 66</a></i> is released for the <a href="/PlayStation_2" title="PlayStation 2">PlayStation 2</a> in North America, along with the album, <i><a href="/Crush_40_(album)" title="Crush 40 (album)">Crush 40</a></i>.</li>
<li><b>2004</b> - The <a href="/Nintendo_GameCube" title="Nintendo GameCube">Nintendo GameCube</a> Okaidoku-ban re-releases of <i><a href="/Sonic_Adventure_2:_Battle" title="Sonic Adventure 2: Battle">Sonic Adventure 2: Battle</a></i> and <i><a href="/Super_Monkey_Ball_2" title="Super Monkey Ball 2">Super Monkey Ball 2</a></i> go on sale in Japan. <i><a href="/Astro_Boy:_Tetsuwan_Atom" class="mw-redirect" title="Astro Boy: Tetsuwan Atom">Astro Boy: Tetsuwan Atom</a></i> and <i><a href="/Sakura_Taisen_Monogatari:_Mysterious_Paris" title="Sakura Taisen Monogatari: Mysterious Paris">Sakura Taisen Monogatari: Mysterious Paris</a></i> are released for the <a href="/PlayStation_2" title="PlayStation 2">PlayStation 2</a> in Japan.</li>
<li><b>2008</b> - <i><a href="/Sega_Superstars_Tennis" title="Sega Superstars Tennis">Sega Superstars Tennis</a></i> is released in North America for the <a href="/PlayStation_2" title="PlayStation 2">PlayStation 2</a>, <a href="/PlayStation_3" title="PlayStation 3">PlayStation 3</a>, <a href="/Xbox_360" title="Xbox 360">Xbox 360</a>, <a href="/Nintendo_DS" title="Nintendo DS">Nintendo DS</a> and <a href="/Wii" title="Wii">Wii</a>.</li>
<li><b>2010</b> - <i><a href="/Ryu_ga_Gotoku_4:_Densetsu_o_Tsugumono" class="mw-redirect" title="Ryu ga Gotoku 4: Densetsu o Tsugumono">Ryu ga Gotoku 4: Densetsu o Tsugumono</a></i> is released for the <a href="/PlayStation_3" title="PlayStation 3">PlayStation 3</a> in Japan. <i><a href="/Senjou_no_Valkyria_2_Gallia_Ouritsu_Shikan_Gakkou_Complete_Guide" title="Senjou no Valkyria 2 Gallia Ouritsu Shikan Gakkou Complete Guide">Senjou no Valkyria 2 Gallia Ouritsu Shikan Gakkou Complete Guide</a></i> is published in Japan.</li>
<li><b>2011</b> - <i><a href="/Super_Monkey_Ball_(smartphone)" title="Super Monkey Ball (smartphone)">Super Monkey Ball</a></i> is released for <a href="/Windows_Phone" title="Windows Phone">Windows Phones</a> in North America. <i><a href="/Sega_Mega_Drive_Classic_Collection_Gold_Edition" title="Sega Mega Drive Classic Collection Gold Edition">Sega Mega Drive Classic Collection Gold Edition</a></i> is released for Windows PCs in Europe.</li>
<li><b>2016</b> - <i><a href="/Mario_%26_Sonic_at_the_Rio_2016_Olympic_Games" title="Mario &amp; Sonic at the Rio 2016 Olympic Games">Mario &amp; Sonic at the Rio 2016 Olympic Games</a></i> is released for the <a href="/Nintendo_3DS" title="Nintendo 3DS">Nintendo 3DS</a> in North America.</li></ul>
</div>
