<div class='sfContentBlock'><h2>EMS 3.0 TRANSFORMATION SUMMIT</h2>
<div class="content">
<div class="image"> <img alt="" image="sfImageWrapper" data-sfref="[images|OpenAccessDataProvider]92e7a832-fe31-667a-9799-ff0000a338da" /><span data-sfref="[images|OpenAccessDataProvider]cae4ab32-fe31-667a-9799-ff0000a338da" class="sfImageWrapper"><a href="http://www.naemt.org/events/ems-transformation-summit/ets-program"><img title="Home_BoxedFeature_EMS3_Marcozzi" class="img-responsive" src="/images/default-source/home-page-ads-and-sponsors/home_boxedfeature_ems3_marcozzi.png?sfvrsn=11dcb92_4" alt="Home_BoxedFeature_EMS3_Marcozzi" data-displaymode="Original" /></a></span></div>
<p><strong>Tuesday, April 10, 2018 &ndash;&nbsp;</strong>Hilton Crystal City, Arlington, Virginia&nbsp;</p>
<p>David Marcozzi, MD, MHS-CL, FACEP, has had a diverse and illustrious career in emergency medicine, government, education and the military. On April 10, he will share his expert insights on the state of U.S. healthcare, the top challenges and opportunities ahead, and the role of EMS in the ongoing healthcare transformation. The day's program will include leaders of hospital, fire and private-based EMS agencies&nbsp;who will share details of the practical innovations they have implemented to successfully expand the services they provide. The program is approved for 6 hours of CAPCE credit and includes a luncheon and ample time for Q&amp;A.</p>
