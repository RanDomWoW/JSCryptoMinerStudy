<h2>The Definition of a Data Scientist</h2>
<p>Data science was developed to handle the flood of big data engulfing the world. A blend of statisticians, computer scientists and creative thinkers, data scientists have the:</p>
<ul>
<li>Skills to collect, process and extract value from giant and diverse data sets</li>
<li>Imagination to understand, visualize and communicate their findings to non-data scientists</li>
<li>Ability to create data-driven solutions that boost profits, reduce costs and even help save the world</li>
</ul>
<p>Data scientists work in every industry – from the Defense Department to Internet start-ups to financial institutions – and tackle big data projects on every level.<br />
