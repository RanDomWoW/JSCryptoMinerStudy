<div class="block"><h2>What is Music Therapy?</h2>

<ul>
	<li>Promote Wellness</li>
	<li>Manage Stress</li>
	<li>Alleviate Pain</li>
	<li>Express Feelings</li>
	<li>Enhance Memory</li>
	<li>Improve Communication</li>
	<li>Promote Physical Rehabilitation</li>
