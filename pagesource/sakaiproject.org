<p>Available Now!</p>

<h3>Sakai. Beautiful on every screen.</h3>

<p>Thanks to a responsive design, Sakai looks and works great on every device, from your desktop to your phone.</p>

<div id="fpMovieContainer">
  <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=53RZQVynmMQ" class="lightbox-video" title="Sakai 11 Video"><img src="/sites/default/files/fp-promo-video-img_0.png" alt="Sakai 11 Video"/></a>
</div>

<ul id="fpFeatures">
	<li>
		<div class="fpFeatureImage"><a href="/features#gradebook"><img class="img-responsive" src="/sites/default/files/iconimg-gb-225_0.png" alt="Gradebook" /> </a></div>

		<div class="fpFeatureContent">
			<h4>Grade entry, simplified.</h4>

			<p>Grading is a fundamental part of the teaching experience. Too often, however, this process is complicated by clumsy grading software. In Sakai 11, the Gradebook provides simple, spreadsheet-style grade entry, so you&rsquo;ll be able to grade more easily and efficiently.</p>

			<p><a class="button fpFeatureButton" href="/features#gradebook">Learn More</a></p>
		</div>

		<div class="clearfix">&nbsp;</div>
	</li>
	<li>
		<div class="fpFeatureImage"><a href="/features#morpheus"> <img class="img-responsive" src="/sites/default/files/iconimg-favorites-225.png" alt="Favorites"/> </a></div>

		<div class="fpFeatureContent">
			<h4>Favorite sites, just a click away.</h4>

			<p>Users have always been able to add sites in Sakai to their &ldquo;Favorites&rdquo; bar to make them easily accessible. Now, this process is even easier. Simply star a site to mark a favorite site, at which point it will display at the top of your screen.</p>

			<p><a class="button fpFeatureButton" href="/features#morpheus">Learn More</a></p>
		</div>

		<div class="clearfix">&nbsp;</div>
	</li>
	<li>
		<div class="fpFeatureImage"><a href="/features#lessons"><img class="img-responsive" src="/sites/default/files/iconimg-lessons-225.png" alt="Lessons"/> </a></div>

		<div class="fpFeatureContent">
			<h4>Lessons, enhanced.</h4>

			<p>The Lessons tool, which allows you to construct modules of content for students, is even more powerful in Sakai 11. With an improved user interface and multi-column support, instructors have more flexibility than ever before.</p>

			<p><a class="button fpFeatureButton" href="/features#lessons">Learn More</a></p>
		</div>

		<div class="clearfix">&nbsp;</div>
