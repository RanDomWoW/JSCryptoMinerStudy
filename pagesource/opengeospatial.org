                    <h1 class="page-title">Welcome to the OGC</h1>                                                  <div class="region region-content">
  <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system">

      
  <div class="content">
                        
      
    
  <div class="content">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><p>The OGC (Open Geospatial Consortium) is an international not for profit organization committed to making quality open standards for the global geospatial community. These standards are made through a consensus process and are freely available for anyone to use to improve sharing of the world's geospatial data.</p>
<p>OGC standards are used in a wide variety of domains including Environment, Defense, Health, Agriculture, Meteorology, Sustainable Development and many more.</p>
<p>Our members come from government, commercial organizations, NGOs, academic and research organizations.</p>
<p>Consider becoming a member today and join our dynamic and diverse global standards community. </p>
<p>
</p><hr /><p></p>
<p><a href="http://www.opengeospatial.org/ogc/organization/careers">Career opportunities</a> are available at OGC. </p></div></div></div>  </div>
