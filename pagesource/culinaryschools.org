				<h1>Becoming a Chef </h1>
				<div class="content-inner">

<p>Welcome to  the leading website  in helping 
culinary students find a school and career path to fit their needs and aspirations.  We highlight and 
  review all varieties of cooking schools so<img src="images/big-chef-with-clipboard-v.gif" alt="chef schools" width="109" height="369" align="right" /> you can connect to the training and guidance that allows you to confidently step  into the world of the culinary arts.</p>
<H2>Culinary Degree Paths</H2>
<p>Within the broad field of culinary arts are many subspecialties and potential majors to consider. In addition to deciding where to go, an aspiring chef  also needs to identify what they'd like to study. We review and examine a variety of <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/culinary-majors/">culinary majors</a>, <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/culinary-majors/HRM/">food and beverage management majors</a>, and we encourage you to <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/culinary-degree-types/">compare  different culinary-related degrees</a>.</p>
<h2>Career Information </h2>
<p>We perform independent market research and conduct in-depth interviews to bring you the facts about culinary careers. We  provide you with balanced data and unique, personal insights to encourage bolder decisions. Learn more about becoming a chef today, including <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/chef-types/">types of chefs</a>, <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/career-info/">a chef's career outlook</a>, the <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/career-info/job-market/">culinary job market</a>, and <a href="https://www.culinaryschools.org/career-info/salaries/">expected wages</a>.</p>
