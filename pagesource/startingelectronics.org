		<h2>What You Will Find Here</h2>
		<p>The Starting Electronics website contains tutorials, projects, reviews and articles on electronics, embedded systems, microcontrollers, Arduino, Raspberry PI, tools and related topics. Here you will find information for hobbyists and beginners to advanced users.</p>
		<h2>Electronics for Beginners</h2>
		<p>Beginners start looking in the <a href="beginners">beginner's area</a>. A good place to start learning about electronics is the <a href="/beginners/start-electronics-now">Start Electronics Now!</a> series of tutorials which is an introduction to hobby electronics / breadboard circuits and the Arduino microcontroller board.</p>

Areas and sub-areas can be navigated using the vertical menu and will change to suit each area. Icons on the menu will tell you whether a link is to an area or article.</p>
