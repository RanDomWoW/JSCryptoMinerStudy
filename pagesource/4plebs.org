<div class="intro">
<p class="lead">4plebs is a community run archive of 4chan.org</p>
<p>We archive all threads and images from specific imageboards on 4chan. Our aim is to preserve unique content for future generations (and everything else that comes along with that). Reminder: 4chan is serious business, you might get offended.</p>
<p>The software enables you to browse the boards just like you would on 4chan, enhanced with features like advanced search and statistics. Ghost posting makes it possible to continue the discussion after the thread has been purged from 4chan. This archive is unofficial and is in no way associated with 4chan.</p>
<p>If you are still confused, you should read our <a href="//archive.4plebs.org/_/articles/faq/" title="Frequently asked questions">FAQ</a> page or swing by the <a href="//archive.4plebs.org/plebs/" title="/plebs/ - 4plebs discussion">discussion board</a>.</p>
<p>Click the board you are interested in to go directly to the archive index of that board.</p>
