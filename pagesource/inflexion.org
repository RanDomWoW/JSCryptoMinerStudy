The Inflexion Approach builds on the work of Peter Senge and Michael Fullan and was developed by Inflexion’s Executive Director, <a href="https://www.inflexion.org/team/matt-coleman/">Matt Coleman</a>, who used the framework as a high school administrator to drive decisions resulting in equitable outcomes for students.</p>

Our work at Inflexion supports educational leaders as they focus on these four elements. We believe that a clear, shared organizational identity informs the structures needed to support learning and empowers readiness for all students.</p>
