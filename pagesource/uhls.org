        <p class="body" align="center"><b>UHLS Libraries Get Mobile App</b></p>
        <p class="body">We've got a brand new way to access library resources! 
          Public libraries in Albany &amp; Rensselaer counties now offer an app 
          called <b><a href="http://uhls.boopsie.com/"><i>UHLS Mobile</i></a></b>, 
          available for free in both Android and Apple app stores.<br>
          <br>
          The app features include the ability to search the <a href="http://catalog.uhls.org"><u>UHLS 
          catalog</u></a>; check the status of a library card account; place physical 
          items (such as books, CDs and DVDs) on hold so they can be picked up 
          at the library; discover and access digitally distributed items like 
          ebooks, audiobooks, and digital magazines; and connect users to other 
          information such as library hours and program calendars. The app even 
          can scan the ISBN barcode of any book to see whether our libraries own 
          a copy of a specific title&#151;and place a request for that book right 
          away. Download <i><b><a href="http://uhls.boopsie.com/">UHLS Mobile</a></b></i> 
          today and take us with you wherever you go this summer!</p>
