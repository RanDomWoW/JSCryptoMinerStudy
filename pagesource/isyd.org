<h3></h3>
<h3><span style="color: #999999;">The International School of Yacht Design is a successor of the firm <span style="color: #666699;"><strong>Yacht Research Applications</strong></span> which has taught public yacht design courses in Sweden since 1985. More than 2000 yachtsmen have been educated in the basics of yacht design theory and application.</span></h3>
<h4></h4>
<h4>Target group</h4>
<p>The main target group is yachtsmen interested in understanding the design of their yacht, either to improve the yacht’s performance by modification of the hull, keel, rudder or sail plan, or simply to become a better sailor. The course is also of interest to those who want to learn how to evaluate the performance of a yacht based on its main data, for instance when buying a new yacht. A smaller group of interested course participants is those who ultimately want to design their own yacht.</p>
