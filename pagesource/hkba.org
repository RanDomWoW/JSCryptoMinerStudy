<div class="readmore-text" style="display:none;">
  <p>Welcome to the website of the Hong Kong Bar Association&nbsp;(the "HKBA").</p>
<p>The HKBA&nbsp;is the professional organization of barristers in Hong Kong.&nbsp;Barristers&nbsp;are self-employed independent legal&nbsp;practitioners. &nbsp;We are&nbsp;specialised&nbsp;advocates and legal advisors providing legal services&nbsp;upon instructions by solicitors and other&nbsp;professionals&nbsp;as permitted under the&nbsp;Code of Conduct.</p>
<p>Our conduct is&nbsp;governed by the&nbsp;stringent Code of Conduct. &nbsp;While we are under a duty to act in the best interest of our clients, our overriding duty is to&nbsp;honestly&nbsp;assist the court to do justice. &nbsp;Barristers play an important role in&nbsp;upholding&nbsp;the rule of law in Hong Kong. &nbsp;Therefore, it is important that the work of HKBA can be understood by the community.</p>
<p>Here you will find&nbsp;a wealth of&nbsp;information about&nbsp;the&nbsp;HKBA, its&nbsp;rules and regulations. For those who are interested in the services&nbsp;of barristers, you will find&nbsp;information about&nbsp;the&nbsp;individual barristers,&nbsp;the legal&nbsp;services&nbsp;we can provide and how we provide such services. For those who wish to become barristers, you will find the relevant rules and procedures&nbsp;on admission. &nbsp;This website also serves as the means of communication between the Bar Council and its members. To the general public, you will find&nbsp;our latest news,&nbsp;press releases, submission andposition papers published by the Bar Council.</p>
<p>Thank you for visiting our website.&nbsp;I hope you&nbsp;enjoy browsing&nbsp;it, and&nbsp;find it&nbsp;informative and&nbsp;useful.</p>
<p>Philip J Dykes SC<br />
Chairman</p>
