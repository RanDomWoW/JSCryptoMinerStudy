                        It's time to plan your springtime getaway to Big Sur.<br>
                        <br>
Wildflowers always seem to be blooming, <a href="condors.html">California Condors</a> are flying overhead throughout Big Sur. California <a href="seaotter.html">Sea Otters</a> are rafting in the kelp beds. <br>
<br>
These are just a few of the natural wonders that make Big Sur a great place to visit. Come on out and watch the natural magic unfold before your eyes, and create your own.</font></p>

                        Big Sur is a special place to many people. To help keep it as pristine as possible and to ensure your own safety, please observe these <a href="rules.html">sustainable practices</a> and follow these</font><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica"> rules of the road and basic safety tips.</font>                        </p>
                        <p><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica"><strong>American National Scenic Byway &amp; California Scenic Highway</strong><br>
                        <img src="images2/04ca-hwy.jpg" width="58" height="84" align="left" border="0" alt="California Scenic Highway" hspace="4"><img src="images2/04-images/AmericasByways_tucked.color.gif" width="130" height="70" align="right" border="1" alt="America's Byways">Highway 1 through Big Sur is a designated <a href="scenic_byways.html" onClick="javascript:urchinTracker('/outgoing/byways_orgbrowse/byways/2301/overview.html');" title="American National Scenic Byway in Big Sur California">American National Scenic </a><a href="scenic_byways.html" onClick="javascript:urchinTracker('/outgoing/byways_orgbrowse/byways/2301/overview.html');" title="American National Scenic Byway in Big Sur California">Byway</a> &amp; California Scenic Highway, an honor reserved for highways that are so distinctive they are destinations unto themselves. Call (888) 836-0866 for information about traffic delays and road closures.</span></font></p>
<p class="class"><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica"><strong>Monterey Bay National Marine Sanctuary </strong><br>
                          The <a href="http://montereybay.noaa.gov/">Monterey Bay National Marine Sancturary</a> is a Federally protected marine area offshore of California's central coast. Stretching from Marin to Cambria the MBNMS encompasses a shoreline length of 276 miles and 4,601 square nautical miles of ocean.</font></p>
                        <p class="class"><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica">Big Sur, offering the best lodging in Monterey County and the best lodging in California. Big Sur also offers the &quot;Best Restaurant in California.&quot;</font></p>
                        <p class="class"><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica">From Big Sur luxury <a href="lodging.html" title="Big Sur hotels">hotels</a> and spas to rustic creekside <a href="camping.html" title="Big Sur camping">campgrounds</a> we believe Big Sur offers <u>The Best</u> in a wide variety of possibilities and we think you'll agree. Join us for a romantic getaway and come share with us one of the most spectacular coastlines in the world.</font></p>
                        <p><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica"><strong>Things to do in Big Sur</strong></font>                        
                        <p><font size="-1" face="arial"><a href="campfire-program.html">State Parks Activities</a><br>
                        Pfeiffer Big Sur State Park has an active guided hikes, Junior Rangers, and Ranger Cubs programs through the summer months; Memorial Day through Labor Day. A series of activities for the whole family. Park day use fees apply for Nature Walks and daytime activities.<br>
                        </font><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica"><br>
                          <a title="Local Hiking Trails - Things to do in Big Sur" href="hiking-trails.html">Local Hiking</a><br>
                          From beaches to mountains... Hike along the streams in the cool, tree lined valleys. Climb up on the high ridges for a spectacular view of the coastline on the western slope and gaze into three million acres of wilderness in the Los Padres National Forest on the eastern slope.<br>
                        <br>
                          Evenings offer the opportunity to dine in <a href="restaurants.html">restaurants</a> from fanciful to exquisite. Relax in lodging that ranges from rustic to luxurious. Camp out in the many well equipped campgrounds. Luxuriate at the local health spas. And of course one of the favorite past-times of Big Sur, <i>Do Nothing!</i></font>
                        <p><font face="verdana,ariel,helvetica">Check the <a href="events.html">Calendar of Events</a> page for local <span style="font-size: 10pt">music, whale watching, festivals, marathons, lighthouse tours and many other events.</span><br>
                          <br>
                          <strong>Do Nothing!</strong><br>
                        That's right. Relax and take in the magnificent beauty of Big Sur. Once you are here there is no reason to do anything more. Replenish your spirit by simply absorbing the weepingly beautiful vistas of Big Sur. <i>Do Nothing</i> in Big Sur and leave refreshed and rejuvenated from head to toe. Big Sur has been here for millions of years. We don't have that luxury. Make your reservation today!</font>
