            <div property="schema:text"><h2>Changing Medicine. Changing Lives.<sup>®</sup></h2>

<p>University of Iowa Health Care (UI Health Care) represents the integrated health-care enterprise of the University of Iowa, which includes:</p>

<ul><li><a href="https://uihc.org/">UI Hospitals and Clinics</a></li>
	<li><a href="http://www.uichildrens.org/">UI Stead Family Children's Hospital</a></li>
	<li><a href="http://medicine.uiowa.edu/">UI Roy J. and Lucille A. Carver College of Medicine</a></li>
	<li><a href="http://www.medicine.uiowa.edu/uiphysicians/">UI Physicians</a></li>
</ul><p>UI Health Care is committed to excellence in:</p>

<h2><a href="/education">Education</a></h2>

<p>Through its mission, UI Health Care trains the next generation of leaders in patient care, medical research, and education.</p>

<ul><li><a href="http://gme.medicine.uiowa.edu/">Graduate Medical Education</a></li>
</ul><h2><a href="http://www.medicine.uiowa.edu/research">Research</a></h2>

<p>Our research has led to some of the most important discoveries in treatments for cancer, heart attack, stroke, eye diseases, hearing loss, genetic diseases, birth defects, and many other conditions.</p>

<h2><a href="https://uihc.org/primary-and-specialty-care">Patient Care and Service</a></h2>

<p>Comprehensive multidisciplinary care is a hallmark of an academic medical center. The full spectrum of specialty and subspecialty care draws patients from Iowa, the region, and around the world.</p>

<ul><li><a href="https://uihc.org/nursing-iowa">Department of Nursing Services and Patient Care</a></li>
</ul></div>
