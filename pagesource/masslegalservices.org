    <h2>Welcome to MassLegalServices.org, the online poverty law library and collaboration resource for legal services advocates in Massachusetts.</h2>

<div class="quicklinks quicklinks-page">

<p><a href="http://www.massprobono.org" target="_blank">MassProBono</a></p>

<p><a href="/LegalAdvocacyGuides" target="_self">Legal Advocacy Guides</a></p>

<p>MassLegalServices offers resources and information about legal issues facing lower income Massachusetts residents to legal services advocates and social services professionals. If you are not a lawyer or legal advocate, you will probably find <a href="http://www.masslegalhelp.org">MassLegalHelp</a> more useful.</p>

<p>To see if free legal representation for your civil legal problem is available, use the <a href="/findlegalaid" target="_blank">Massachusetts Legal Resource Finder</a>.</p>

<p>If you are an attorney looking for a pro bono opportunity, see <a href="http://www.massprobono.org" target="_blank">www.MassProBono.org</a> -- the website for pro bono attorneys and volunteers in Massachusetts.</p>
