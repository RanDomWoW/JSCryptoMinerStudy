						<h1 id="resurrect-intro-heading">Join us on our Journey to Heaven</h1>
			
						<div id="resurrect-intro-content">
				<p><img class="alignleft wp-image-4306" src="/wp-content/uploads/2017/04/Pastorwfside_1.png" alt="Pastor W.F Kumuyi" width="239" height="169" /></p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; line-height: normal;">Dearly beloved,</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; line-height: normal;">I am delighted to warmly welcome you to our homepage. You have come to the right place at the right time. Here, we are very passionate about your eternal destiny and committed to your spiritual welfare.  This is a website that has a lot to offer you – with unlimited access to life-transforming, systematic and expository teaching of the word of God and resources specifically tailored to meet your own spiritual needs; deepen your walk with God and add value to your entire life.</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; line-height: normal;">Our numerous resources are carefully prepared with you in mind- to satisfy your spiritual hunger; enhance your spiritual growth and; equip you for divine service, all round success and victory over the challenges of life.</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; line-height: normal;">You can now plunge into the great riches of the Kingdom that have been preserved for you via the hyperlinks to pages on  downloadable sermons, Bible studies, target-group  resources, gospel literature and magazines, leadership and church growth materials and faith-building testimonies etc. You can equally link up with us via this site for live streaming/broadcast of our regular refreshing worship and study sessions as well as soul-stirring, power-packed special revival services.</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: arial; font-size: 20px; text-align: justify; line-height: normal;">It is now my greatest delight to invite you to commit the rest of your life to the Great Saviour, Jesus Christ, who died for our sins- to accept Him as your personal Lord and Saviour; to enjoy the warmth of our fellowship, and then travel along with us on this highway of holiness to our eternal home in heaven.</p>
