<div class="double-column-left">
<h3 class="grey-header">About the Yale Student Employment Office</h3>

<p>The Yale Student Employment Office (SEO) provides assistance to both students and employers in meeting their employment needs. SEO provides free job posting and on-line application services to the campus and local community. In addition, SEO assists students in completing their employment paperwork and provides satellite payroll services for students. The office also provides electronic timesheet services to the campus.</p>
