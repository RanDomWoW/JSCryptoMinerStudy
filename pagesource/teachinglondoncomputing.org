<p><span style="color:#000000;"><strong>Teaching London Computing</strong> supports Computing teachers in London and beyond  (we hope our resources help you where ever you are) providing workshops and <span style="text-decoration:underline;"><span style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Resources for teachers" href="https://teachinglondoncomputing.org/resources/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">free classroom resources</a></span></span>.  It is a resource Hub for <span style="text-decoration:underline;"><span style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" href="http://www.computingatschool.org.uk/crcs/london">CAS London</a></span></span>: the Computing at School Regional Centre.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Our model of delivery is that we provide resources and workshops, and help teachers to do the same. Those who use them then share them with other teachers so spreading the understanding and reinforcing the learning. Other teachers can provide a supportive environment to improve your understanding.</span></p>
<p style="text-align:center;"><span style="color:#000000;"><strong><em>The best way to learn is to teach other teachers!</em></strong></span></p>

<h2><span style="color:#000000;">Who are we?</span></h2>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Teaching London Computing is a partnership between</span> <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Queen Mary University of London" href="http://www.qmul.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Queen Mary University of London</a></span> <span style="color:#000000;">and</span> <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="KCL - King's College, London" href="http://www.kcl.ac.uk" target="_blank" rel="noopener">King&#8217;s College London</a></span>.</p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">We have funding through the  London Schools Excellence Fund and the London Schools Excellence Legacy Fund from the <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Mayor of London - Greater London Authority" href="http://london.gov.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Mayor of London</a></span> and the <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Department for Education - gov.uk" href="http://www.education.gov.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Department for Education</a></span> (2013-2017) and we work closely with <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Computing At School" href="http://www.computingatschool.org.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Computing At School</a></span>.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">Google has provided extra support for our work through their <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Google: CS4HS" href="http://www.cs4hs.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">CS4HS programme</a></span>.</span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">We are also working with</span> <span style="color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" title="Hertford College, University of Oxford" href="http://www.hertford.ox.ac.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Hertford College, University of Oxford</a></span> <span style="color:#000000;">on Interdisciplinary Computing resources. </span></p>
<p><span style="color:#000000;">A range of our resources have now been integrated in the national <span style="text-decoration:underline;color:#0000ff;"><a style="color:#0000ff;text-decoration:underline;" href="https://www.computingatschool.org.uk/tenderfoot/">Tenderfoot programme </a></span>training teachers.</span></p>
