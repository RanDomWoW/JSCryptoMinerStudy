      <div class="featured-text">
            <h3 class="font3">
              <a href="aa2017.html">Academy Awards - Oscar Winners: </a> </h3>
            <p class="font4"> AMPAS' Oscars (Academy Awards) have now concluded
              their 90th anniversary. The major Best Picture Oscar winner was
              Mexican film-maker Guillermo del Toro's 
              <i>The Shape of Water </i>(with 13 nominations
              and 4 wins - Best Picture, Best Director, Best Original Score,
              and Best Production Design) - a fantasy about the romance
              between a mute woman (Sally Hawkins) and a misunderstood, imprisoned
              amphibious man-monster (Doug Jones). The other three top films
              included Christopher Nolan's WWII war epic <i>Dunkirk</i> (with
              8 nominations and 3 wins in technical categories), director Joe
              Wright's UK film <i>Darkest Hour</i> (with 6 nominations and 2
              wins, including Gary Oldman's Best Actor win for his realistic
              portrayal of Winston Churchill), and Martin McDonagh's crime drama <i>Three
              Billboards outside Ebbing, Missouri</i> (with 7 nominations and
              2 wins, including two performance Oscars for Frances McDormand
              and Sam Rockwell). See all the details here! </font><br />
            </p>
