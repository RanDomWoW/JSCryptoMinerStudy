<h1>Invitation</h1>
<p>Welcome to the 2018 International Conference on Radar, the world’s premier international conference on radar systems.  Radar returns to Australia in 2018 and, for the first time, it will be held in the city of Brisbane.</p>
<p>Our tagline this year is “New Frontiers in Radar”.  We aim to live up to this tagline with an exciting array of cutting-edge plenary lectures, tutorials and special sessions.  Peer-reviewed oral and poster presentations will take us to the research frontiers.  There will be an exhibition featuring latest radar technology as well as opportunities for networking.  Check back regularly for the latest updates or sign up for email bulletins.</p>
<p>The venue will be the award-winning Brisbane Convention &amp; Exhibition Centre, located in the heart of the city’s cultural, entertainment and dining precinct, Southbank.  August in Brisbane is a marvellous time to visit.  Temperatures are mild and August boasts the least number of rain days and the highest number of clear days in the year.</p>
<p>I look forward to seeing you in Brisbane for Radar ’18.</p>
<p>Vaughan Clarkson<br />
 General Chair</p>
