            <h5>The SIFMA Foundation</h5>
            <p>
                An independent 501(c)3 educational organization dedicated to fostering knowledge of the financial markets,
                serving 16 million students since 1977.
            </p>
        </li>
        <li>
            <a href="http://www.sifma.org/foundation" class="connector-line">
                <img src="img/smg_circlelogo.png" alt="Stock Market Game"/>
            </a>
            <h5>Stock Market Game<sup>&#8482;</sup></h5>
            <p>
                An online simulation of the global capital markets that engages students grades 4-12 in the world of
                economics, investing and personal finance and that has prepared 16 million students for financially independent futures.
            </p>
        </li>
        <li>
            <a href="http://www.investwrite.org" class="connector-line">
                <img src="img/INVESTwrite_logo.png" alt="INVESTwrite"/>
            </a>
            <h5>InvestWrite<sup>&reg;</sup></h5>
            <p>
                A national essay competition that serves as a culminating activity for Stock Market
                Game<sup>&#8482;</sup> students.
            </p>
        </li>
        <li>
            <a href="capitol-hill-challenge.html" class="connector-line">
                <img src="img/CHC_logo.png" alt="Capitol Hill Challenge"/>
            </a>
            <h5>Capitol Hill Challenge<sup>&#8482;</sup></h5>
            <p>
                A national financial education competition for junior high and high schools that reaches all 50
                U.S. states and all members of Congress.
            </p>
        </li>
        <li>
            <a href="https://investitforward.sifma.org/">
                <img src="img/INVEST_logo.png" alt="Invest it Forward"/>
            </a>
            <h5>Invest It Forward<sup>&#8482;</sup></h5>
            <p>
                An industry-wide financial education and capital markets literacy campaign convening
                volunteers from hundreds of financial firms.
            </p>
        </li>
    </ul>
