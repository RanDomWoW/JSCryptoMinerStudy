  <div id="features" class="block">
    <h2>features</h2>

    <h3>sky</h3>
    <ul class="smalllist">
      <li>default catalogue of over 600,000 stars</li>
      <li>extra catalogues with more than 177 million stars</li>
      <li>default catalogue of over 80,000 deep-sky objects</li>
      <li>extra catalogue with more than 1 million deep-sky objects</li>
      <li>asterisms and illustrations of the constellations</li>
      <li>constellations for 20+ different cultures</li>
      <li>images of nebulae (full Messier catalogue)</li>
      <li>realistic Milky Way</li>
      <li>very realistic atmosphere, sunrise and sunset</li>
      <li>the planets and their satellites</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>interface</h3>
    <ul class="smalllist">
      <li>a powerful zoom</li>
      <li>time control</li>
      <li>multilingual interface</li>
      <li>fisheye projection for planetarium domes</li>
      <li>spheric mirror projection for your own low-cost dome</li>
      <li>all new graphical interface and extensive keyboard control</li>
      <li>telescope control</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>visualisation</h3>
    <ul class="smalllist">
      <li>equatorial and azimuthal grids</li>
      <li>star twinkling</li>
      <li>shooting stars</li>
      <li>tails of comets</li>
      <li>iridium flares simulation</li>
      <li>eclipse simulation</li>
      <li>supernovae and novae simulation</li>
      <li>3D sceneries</li>
      <li>skinnable landscapes, now with spheric panorama projection</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>customizability</h3>
    <ul class="smalllist">
      <li>plugin system adding artifical satellites, ocular simulation, telescope configuration and more</li>
      <li>ability to add new solar system objects from online resources...</li>
      <li>add your own deep sky objects, landscapes, constellation images, scripts...</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

    <h2>system requirements</h2>

    <h3>minimal</h3>
    <ul class="smalllist">
      <li>Linux/Unix; Windows 7 and above; Mac OS X 10.10.0 and above</li>
      <li>3D graphics card which supports OpenGL 3.0 and GLSL 1.3</li>
      <li>512 MiB RAM</li>
      <li>250 MiB on disk</li>
    </ul>

    <h3>recommended</h3>
    <ul class="smalllist">
      <li>Linux/Unix; Windows 7 and above; Mac OS X 10.11.0 and above</li>
      <li>3D graphics card which supports OpenGL 3.3 and above</li>
      <li>1 GiB RAM or more</li>
      <li>1.5 GiB on disk</li>
    </ul>
