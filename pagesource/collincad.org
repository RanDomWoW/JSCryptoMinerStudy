	<h2>
					<a href="/9-announcements/169-2018-protest-deadline-changes">
			Property Tax Protest Filing Deadline Changes for 2018</a>
			</h2>





 <dl class="article-info">
 <dt class="article-info-term">Details</dt>
		<dd class="published">
		Published on Wednesday, 10 January 2018 08:11		</dd>
 </dl>


<div class="article_body">



Due to passage of HB 2228 during the 2017 Texas Legislature, changes were made to the property tax protest filing deadline; the protest deadline was changed to May 15 from May 31, or 30 days after the delivery of the Notice of Appraised Value, whichever is later. Property owners and agents should start receiving appraisal notices, from Collin Central Appraisal District, for the 2018 tax year around April 15<sup>th</sup>. 


</div> <!-- END div.article_body // -->

 <dt class="article-info-term">Details</dt>
		<dd class="published">
		Published on Thursday, 23 July 2015 10:50		</dd>
 </dl>


<div class="article_body">
