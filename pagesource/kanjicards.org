	<li><a href="/kanji-lists.html">Lists of kanji</a> according to <acronym title="Japanese Language Proficiency Test">JLPT</acronym>-level, Japanese school grades and the frequency of use.</li>
	<li>A script to generate <a href="/generate-list.html">individualized kanji-lists</a>.</li>
	<li>For more than 3000 kanji all Information required to write your own kanji study cards. This includes the readings and meaning of the kanji as well as the stroke order and example-words</li>
	<li>A download section with <a href="/pdf-files.html">pdf-files</a> with kanji cards for printing.</li>
</ul>
