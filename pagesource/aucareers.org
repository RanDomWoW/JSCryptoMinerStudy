<h2 class="headings">Employment Categories</h2>
<p>AUC recognizes that its prime resources are its employees and it depends on their commitment 
and efforts for its continued prosperity and growth. AUC shall recruit, appoint, 
upgrade and develop those persons who best meet the requirements of a vacant position and who are able to contribute to the success of the Organisation through a comprehensive,
 pro-active 
and positive concept. 
<img src="images/body_images/agenda.jpg" alt="africanunionphoto" style="padding-right: 20px;float:right;padding-left:5%!important;padding-top:5%!important;"/>
The African Union Commission is an equal opportunity employer which upholds the rights of all candidates who apply and in the spirit of equal opportunity
 strongly encourages 
qualified African women as well as citizens of least represented countries to apply.</p>
<h2>Internationally Recruited Positions</h2>
<p>For Internationally recruited positions AUC will always post such jobs on the careers website as well as Facebook, Twitter and LinkedIn. Please follow our pages on these social media
so as to be notified if such positions are available.</p>
<h2>Headquarters Internship Programme</h2><p>AUC provides opportunities for students enrolled in a graduate programme to undertake an Internship at
its Headquarters in Addis Ababa. Only applicants with a minimum First Degree will be considered. 
<img src="images/body_images/internship.jpg" alt="africanunionphoto" style="float:left;padding-right:5%!important;padding-top:5%!important;"/>
Please note that Internship within the Commission do not attract any
form of remuneration. To apply for Internship please follow this link <a href="http://www.internship.aucareers.org/" style="color: #008000"  target="_blank">Apply for Internship.</a></p>
<h2>Locally recruited positions</strong></h2><p>For jobs in the General Service and related categories that offers local contracts, 
including those in the security and safety, secretarial and other support positions, please check the AU career website and also contact the local office of the AUC in your country directly.</p><br/><br/>

<h2 class="headings">Strategic directions</h2>
<p class="mission"><b>The Vision</b></p>
<br/>
<p>The vision of the African Union is that of: <b><i>"An integrated, prosperous and peaceful Africa, driven by its own citizens and

<h2 class="headings">What are the various employment profiles of our staff</h2>
<p>AUC recruits staff on fixed-term, temporary basis or on a regular basis as when the need arises. Staff are grouped into two broad categories, Professional 
and General Service staff categories.</p><br/>
<ol style="list-style-type: none;">
<li><p><b>Elected Officials (Group 1)</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>Professional Staff (Group II)</b></p></li>
<p>This category constitutes staff members responsible, among other things, for conceiving, planning, 
managing, supervising, monitoring, drafting and evaluating departmental activities.
<img src="images/body_images/body_initial.png" alt="africanunionphoto"  style="padding-left:5%;padding-top:5%;float:right;">
These comprise of technical staff 
who are responsible for, among other things, developing, implementing and supervising programme activities and staff members 
with specialized functions, such as revising, translating, interpreting, proof-reading, précis-writing and evaluating materials.</p>
</ol>
<ol style="list-style-type: none;">
<li><p><b>Political and Special Appointees</b></p></li>
<li><p><b>General Service Staff (Group IV)</b></p></li>
<ul style="list-style-type: none;">
<p>The General Service Staff category comprises of two groups</p>
<li><p><b>First Category (GSA)</b></p></li>
<p>Administrative, Clerical, Maintenance and Paramedical.</p>
<li><p><b>Second Category (GSB)</b></p></li>
<p>An Auxiliary to the first category and includes general service staff members responsible among other things for performing routine duties of maintenance.</p>
</ul>
</ol>
<ol style="list-style-type: none;">
<li><p><b>Other category</b></p></li>
<p>Staff members on Short-term Contracts, Field Mission, Project and consultants.</p>
</ol>

<div id="tabs-5">
<h2 class="headings">What are the various types of appointments that we offer</h2>
<ol style="text-align:left;list-style: none;">
<li class="mission"><b >Regular Appointments</b></li> 
<p>These are appointments made in the first instance on a fixed-term contract for a period of three (3) years, the first twelve (12) months 
of which shall be considered probationary period and thereafter for renewable periods every two years subject to performance.</p>
<li class="mission"><b >Continuing Regular Appointments</b></li>
<p>These are appointments made by the Organization to Staff Members who have satisfactorily completed five (5) years of service
 as Regular Staff. 
  The Organization shall ensure that the number of Continuing Staff Members shall not 
 exceed fifty-one percent (51%) of the total number of regular staff of the Organization for each staff category taking into account
 the Staff Member country quotas.</p>
<li class="mission"><b >Fixed-term Appointments</b></li>
<p>These are appointments made for a period not exceeding two years but not less than a year with 
terms and conditions of service specified in their contracts in accordance with AU Polity on recruitment.</p>
<li class="mission"><b >Short-term Appointment</b></li>
<p>These are appointments made for a period not exceeding one year but less than 
three (3) months with terms and conditions of service specified in the contract in accordance with AU Policy on recruitment.</p>
<li><b class="mission">Consultancy</b></li>
<p>These are contracts for a period not exceeding three (3) months with terms and
 conditions of service specified in the contracts in accordance with AU Policy on recruitment.
 <img src="images/body_images/auflag.png" alt="africanunionphoto"  style="padding-left:2%;padding-top:2%;float:right;">
</p>
 <li class="mission"><b >Special Services Appointment</b></li>
<p>These are contracts of a limited duration and are renewable as and when necessary but not conferring on a staff member any full time commitment during its duration. The terms and conditions of service specified in the contracts in accordance with AU Policy on recruitment.</p>
<li class="mission"><b >Political and Special Appointments</b></li>
<p>These are appointments made by Elected Officials for a duration not exceeding their term of office. The Political Appointee shall possess adequate knowledge of the working of the Union.</p>
<li class="mission"><b >General Service Categories</b></li>
<ul style="list-style: none;">
<li class="mission"><p>First Category (GSA), these include Administrative, clerical, maintenance and paramedical staff.</p></li>
<li class="mission"><p>Second Category (GSB),This is an auxiliary to the First category. They are responsible for performing routine duties of maintenance.</p></li>
</ul>
</ol>

<h2>How do I apply</h2>
<p>You are strongly encouraged to apply only to an advertised vacancy notice for which you consider you are qualified and ensure you send the application before the deadline. Upon completion of the personal history form, online applications will be acknowledged via e-mail.
To start the online application process, applicants are required to register online for a personal account.
After opening the account, applicants may apply for vacancies using the AUC personal history form provided.
<img src="images/body_images/contracts.png" alt="africanunionphoto" style="padding-right:2%;padding-top:2%;float:left;"><img src="images/body_images/apply.png" alt="africanunionphoto" style="padding-left:2%;float:right;">
 Once the personal history form has been completed for a particular vacancy, it can be saved and used for future applications. The personal history form may be modified as necessary for future applications.</p>
<p>Please check your qualifications against the requirements displayed in the position description since only those applicants that meet the requirements will be considered. 
The Commission requests all applicants from AU Member States to always apply whenever their exists vacancies that they believe they are qualified for.</p> 

<p>All applications must be received before the vacancy notice closing deadline.</p> 
<p>Once an application has been submitted, it will be transmitted to the appropriate office for review.</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
<ul  style="list-style-type:none!important;">
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>Where is African Union Commission headquarters located ?</b></p></li>
<div style="width:50%!important;">
<iframe src="https://www.google.com/maps/embed?pb=!1m18!1m12!1m3!1d7881.4048810625445!2d38.736314829306444!3d8.999503865670798!2m3!1f0!2f0!3f0!3m2!1i1024!2i768!4f13.1!3m3!1m2!1s0x164b85d4ac95784d%3A0x602e877ba520a5ba!2sAfrican+Union!5e0!3m2!1sen!2set!4v1450090060382" width="600" height="450" frameborder="0" style="border:0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
<div>
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>I want to work for the AUC. Where can I send my application ?</b></p></li>
<p>To apply, please visit our online recruitment database <a href="http://hrm.aucareers.org/erec" style="color: #008000"  target="_blank">E-recruitment</a>
page where you can then create your Profile and submit your application online. The Commission request all applicants from 
AU Member States to send online detailed CVs in accordance with the format indicated.  
The Commission further encourages all applicants to forward their respective application directly to the address specified above.</p>
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>I have dual nationality status, which nationality will be considered for employment ?</b></p></li>
<p>In the application form, specify preferred nationality as the nationality you want to be considered for, however you should have supporting papers notably
a passport confirming your citizenship of the preferred nationality country.</p>
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>Can I apply for an internship ?</b></p></li>
<p>The African Union provides opportunities for students enrolled in a graduate programme to undertake an internship at its Headquarters in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.
To apply for internship please follow this <a href="http://www.internship.aucareers.org/" style="color: #008000"  target="_blank">link</a>.</p>
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>As an Intern, Can I receive financial assistance from the AUC ?</b></p></li>
<p>Financial aid is not available to interns through the AUC.</p>
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>How can I contribute to the work of the AUC ?</b></p></li>
<p>The Organization accepts voluntary financial contributions if consistent with its aims and activities.</p>
<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>I have a project to advance the goals of the African Union. Does the AUC offer financial, logistical or legal assistance ?</b></p></li>
<p>No. Being an Organization of sovereign states, its resources are allocated only to programmes which have been officially approved by its members. However, there are several ways Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) can collaborate with the AU on its activities.
</p>
</ul>

<li style="list-style-type:none!important;"><p><b>Does the AUC offer scholarships ?</b></p></li>
<p>The AU offers no general scholarship or student exchange programme.</p>
