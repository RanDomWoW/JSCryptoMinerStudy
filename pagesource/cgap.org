<h2 class="pane-title"><span class="title-first">About</span> <span class="title-second">CGAP</span></h2>
<div class="pane-content">
<div id='boxes-box-about_cgap' class='boxes-box'><div class="boxes-box-content"><p>The Consultative Group to Assist the Poor is a global partnership of more than 30 leading organizations that seek to advance financial inclusion. CGAP develops innovative solutions through practical research and active engagement with financial service providers, policy makers, and funders to enable approaches at scale. Housed at the World Bank, CGAP combines a pragmatic approach to responsible market development with an evidence-based advocacy platform to increase access to the financial services the poor need to improve their lives.</p>
