<h2><a href="http://www.adlit.org/strategies/22093/">Question Generation</a></h2>

<p>a strategy that assists students with their comprehension of text. Students learn to formulate and respond to questions about situations, facts, and ideas while engaged in understanding a text. During this process, there are several different types of questions that may be derived.<br />
