<h2>Welcome</h2>
<p>The Environmental Literacy Council is an independent, non-profit made up of scientists, economists, and educators striving to connect teachers and students to science-based information on environmental issues. Our website offers over 1000 pages of background information and resources on environmental topics, along with curricular materials, and textbook reviews.</p>
<h3><a href="/teachers-index.php">For Teachers</a></h3>
<p>In our area just for teachers, we have lesson plans and labs; tips and handouts from experienced teachers; and expert reviews of the textbooks you use everyday. </p>
<h3><a href="/students-index.php">For Students</a></h3>
<p>Get homework help and learn more about environmental topics like energy use, climate change, and recycling. Don?t miss our <a href="/category.php/21.html">Special Features</a> describing cool creatures, neat places, and environmental topics in the news.</p>
