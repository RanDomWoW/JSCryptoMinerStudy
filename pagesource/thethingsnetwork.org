                <h2 class="fat-blue left">BUILDING A FULLY DISTRIBUTED INTERNET OF THINGS DATA INFRASTRUCTURE.</h2>
                <div class="wrapper-video-text">
                    <p>
                        
                            The Things Network is building a network for the Internet of Things by creating abundant data connectivity, so applications and businesses can flourish.<br /><br/>
                            The technology we use is called LoRaWAN and it allows for things to talk to the internet without 3G or WiFi. So no WiFi codes and no mobile subscriptions.<br /><br/>
                            It features low battery usage, long range and low bandwidth. Perfect for the internet of things.
                        
                    </p>
