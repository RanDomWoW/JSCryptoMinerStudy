		<h1 class="entry-title">Welcome to WWOOF</h1>
					
	</header><!-- /entry-header -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="font-size: 1.2em;"><strong>WWOOF organisations</strong> connect people who want to live and learn on organic farms and smallholdings with people who want to share their lifestyles, teach new skills and welcome volunteer help. There are places in Africa, Americas, Asia, Europe, Middle East and Oceania.</p>
<p style="font-size: 1.2em;"><strong>WWOOF hosts</strong> offer food, accommodation and opportunities to stay and learn about organic/biological growing and farming.<br />
<strong>WWOOF Volunteers</strong> give hands on help and have an interest in learning about organic farming and gaining skills in sustainable ways of living.</p>
<p style="font-size: 1.2em;"><strong>WWOOF is a network of national organisations</strong>. They have local knowledge and up to date information about WWOOF volunteering in their country. To become involved in the WWOOF community either at home or away explore the drop down menu above or list below and connect with the local WWOOF organisation directly.</p>
<p style="font-size: 1.2em;">For hosts in countries with no WWOOF organisation check out <span style="color: #53bc00;"><strong><a style="color: #53bc00;" href="http://wwoofinternational.org/independents">WWOOF Independents</a> </strong></span>and for Independent hosts in the Asia/Pacific/Middle East go to <strong><span style="color: #53bc00;"><a style="color: #53bc00;" href="http://www.wwoof.asia">WWOOF Asia</a></span></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="font-size: 1.2em;">Make a difference! Get involved! Join WWOOF! Think global &#8211; act local!</p>
