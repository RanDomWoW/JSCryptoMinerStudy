    <div class="content clearfix">
      <div class="view view-digital-library-selections view-id-digital_library_selections view-display-id-block view-dom-id-d7e27e6d1d3b1345c85799e0d79c88b4">

      <p class="intro">ACM SIGGRAPH members enjoy unlimited access to the SIGGRAPH portion of the Digital Library.</p>

      <div class="view-content">
          <div class="dl-row first odd">
      
      <h5 class="frontl-title">
  
    
      <a href="/member-log-in?mem=member"> BundleFusion: Real-Time Globally Consistent 3D Reconstruction Using On-the-Fly Surface Reintegration</a>
      </h5>
  

      <p class="frontl-headline">
  
    
      This article published in the June 2017 issue of ACM TOG contributes a parallelizable optimization framework for real-time, high-quality, 3D scanning of large-scale scenes.

      </p>
  
    </div>
      <div class="dl-row even">
      
      <h5 class="frontl-title">
  
    
      <a href="/member-log-in?mem=member">Real-Time Geometry, Albedo, and Motion Reconstruction Using a Single RGB-D Camera</a>
      </h5>
  

      <p class="frontl-headline">
  
    
      This article published in the June 2017 issue of ACM TOG proposes a real-time method that uses a single-view RGB-D input to simultaneously reconstruct a scene with a detailed geometry model, surface albedo, per-frame non-rigid motion, and per-frame low-frequency lighting.

      </p>
  
    </div>
      <div class="dl-row last odd">
      
      <h5 class="frontl-title">
  
    
      <a href="/member-log-in?mem=member">Boundary Element Octahedral Fields in Volumes</a>
      </h5>
  

      <p class="frontl-headline">
  
    
      This article published in the June 2017 issue of ACM TOG presents an algorithm for computing smooth fields of orthogonal directions within a volume, a critical step in hexahedral mesh generation, used to guide placement of edges and singularities.

      </p>
