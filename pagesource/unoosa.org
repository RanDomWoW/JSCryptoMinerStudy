<h2 style="text-align: justify;">UNISPACE+50</h2>
<p>The year 2018 will mark the fiftieth anniversary of the first UNISPACE conference, held in Vienna in 1968. 
<a href="/oosa/en/ourwork/copuos/current.html">COPOUS</a> decided in June 2015 to use this milestone anniversary to renew and strengthen its mandate as a unique platform for interrelationship between major space faring nations and emerging space nations, supported by UNOOSA.</p>
<p>
  <a class="button-read-more" href="/oosa/en/ourwork/unispaceplus50/index.html">Read more</a>
</p>

<h2>Long-term Sustainability</h2>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The international community seeks to balance access to the benefits of the exploration and peaceful use of outer space with the need to preserve and protect the outer space environment for future generations. A Working Group of the Scientific and Technical Subcommittee is devoted to this theme. </p>
