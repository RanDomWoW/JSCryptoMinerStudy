     <h3>Choosing A Girl's Name</h3>
     The choice of a name may involve several factors and parameters.&nbsp;
     Parents often choose a common name or the name of a favorite family
     member.&nbsp; Others may select an ethnic or religious name, such as
     one with Greek, Hebrew, or Irish origins.&nbsp; Then again, parents
     may wish to give their baby girl an unusual or unique name or name her
     after a famous person.&nbsp; For a real trend-setting name, consider
     <a href="http://abcnews.go.com/WNT/print?id=1977660" target="_blank">Nevaeh</a>,
     ("heaven" spelled backwards), which became the 70th most popular name
     given to baby girls in 2005!
