<p>Having spent years clawing her way to the top of the cutthroat world of public relations, Agatha Rasin is tiring of the high life. She leaves London, opting for early retirement in the picturesque idyll of the Cotswolds. She settles in an outwardly sleepy village and enters a quiche-making competition. But her attempts to curry favor with the locals fails when the judge dies from food poisoning. Agatha begins an amateur investigation into the death.
