      <div class="teaser ctan-tex">
	<h2><span class="t-logo">T<span class="e">e</span>X</span></h2>
        <a href="tex"><img src="/assets/teaser/M/tex-1-bca293031efb62e5747c5b74f65fff6f.png" class="topic-teaser"/></a>
	<p>
	 <span class="t-logo">T<span class="e">e</span>X</span> is a type&shy;set&shy;ting pro&shy;gram de&shy;signed for high-qual&shy;ity com&shy;po&shy;si&shy;tion of
	 ma&shy;te&shy;rial that con&shy;tains a lot of math&shy;e&shy;mat&shy;i&shy;cal and tech&shy;ni&shy;cal ex&shy;pres&shy;sions.
	 It has been adopted by many au&shy;thors and pub&shy;lish&shy;ers who gen&shy;er&shy;ate
	 tech&shy;ni&shy;cal books and pa&shy;pers. It was cre&shy;ated by
	 Pro&shy;fes&shy;sor <a href="http://www-cs-faculty.stanford.edu/~knuth/">Don&shy;ald
	 E.&shy;Knuth</a> of Stan&shy;ford Univer&shy;sity, orig&shy;i&shy;nally for prepa&shy;ra&shy;tion
         of his book se&shy;ries
	 &ldquo;<a href="http://www-cs-faculty.stanford.edu/~knuth/taocp.html">The
	 Art of Com&shy;puter Pro&shy;gram&shy;ming</a>&rdquo;. <span class="t-logo">T<span class="e">e</span>X</span> has been made freely
	 avail&shy;able by Knuth.
	</p>
	<p>
	 From these ori&shy;gins a whole eco-sys&shy;tem of dis&shy;tri&shy;bu&shy;tions, macro pack&shy;ages,
	 and sup&shy;port&shy;ing pro&shy;grams has arisen.
	</p>
	<p class="more"><a href="tex">more</a></p>
