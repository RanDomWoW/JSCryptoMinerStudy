      <section class="featureRichItem">
          <div class="wysiwyg"><p>APA’s 54 divisions are interest groups organized by members. Some represent subdisciplines of psychology (e.g., experimental, social or clinical) while others focus on topical areas such as aging, ethnic-minorities or trauma. APA members, and even non-members, can apply to join one or more divisions, which have their own eligibility criteria and dues. In addition, each division has its own officers, website, publications, email lists, awards, convention activities and meetings.</p></div>
      <div class="more"><div class="wysiwyg"><p><a href="http://www.apa.org/about/division/index.aspx">Learn about all APA divisions</a></p></div></div> 
      </section>

      <section class="featureRichItem">
      <h2>Become a Fellow</h2>                                          
          <div class="wysiwyg"><p>Fellowship is a special distinction for members who have received a doctoral degree in psychology or a related field from a regionally accredited institution and can show evidence of unusual and outstanding contributions in the field of psychology.</p>
<p>Application for fellow status is open to any APA member who meets the criteria of both the sponsoring division and APA. Each division establishes its own criteria for nominations — sometimes the same as APA criteria, but usually involving additional requisites relevant to that division. Each division is responsible for providing the Fellows Committee with updated division criteria.</p></div>
      <div class="more"><div class="wysiwyg"><p><a href="http://www.apa.org/membership/fellows/index.aspx">How to become a fellow</a></p></div></div>

      <h2>Convention Highlights</h2>                                          
          <div class="wysiwyg"><p>The divisions have many events during the <a href="http://www.apa.org/convention/index.aspx" title="APA convention">APA convention</a> each year (taking place Aug. 9-12, 2018, in San Francisco). You can visit the Division Services booth at convention in the APA Member Resources Area. In the booth you can pick up division ribbons to wear on your name badge, join divisions onsite, enter our raffles for prizes, get the location of division hospitality suites and talk with leaders of the divisions face to face. Follow <a href="https://twitter.com/apa_divisions" title="APA Division on Twitter" target="_blank">@APA_Divisions</a> to learn more.</p></div>
