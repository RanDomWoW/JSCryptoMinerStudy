        <h1>About the International Ammunition Association</h1>
        <p><img class="smalldevicespic" src="images/rotate/rotate.php" alt="Selection of Collectors Cartridges" /></p>
        <p>The IAA was founded in 1955 to serve cartridge collectors and professionals in the areas of ammunition research and forensics.</p>
<p> IAA membership includes a subscription to <strong><em>The International Ammunition Journal</em></strong>, a bimonthly magazine wherein you will find: </p>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Featured articles</strong>- on a broad spectrum of cartridges and ammunition history, frequently the result of previously unpublished research by IAA members.</li>
          <li><strong>Coming Events</strong>- for cartridge shows and the meetings of cartridge associations.</li>
          <li><strong>Questions and Answers</strong>- where readers pose their queries to the staff and membership.</li>
          <li><strong>Industry News</strong>- reports on the ammunition industry, new products and announcements.</li>
          <li><strong>Company Histories</strong>- a historical look at some of the more obscure ammunition manufacturers.</li>
          <li><strong>And more</strong>- on cartridge prices, ammunition-related patents, book reviews, clips and links, sectioned cartridges, headstamps, box labels, announcements, and information from the leading experts in the field.</li>
        </ul>
        <p>The <strong>Journal</strong> is a full-color, well-illustrated publication, 60 pages in length. Lots of material on new cartridges and previously unknown old cartridges, and information available nowhere else.</p>

        <p><strong>The International Ammunition Journal</strong> is essential reading for any serious student of ammunition as a professional, historian, researcher or collector. Available in both printed and digital (PDF) forms.</p>
