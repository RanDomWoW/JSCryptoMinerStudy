<div class="content"><p>Tales of Maj&rsquo;Eyal (ToME) is a free, open source <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roguelike">roguelike</a> RPG, featuring tactical turn-based combat and advanced character building.&nbsp; Play as one of many unique races and classes in the lore-filled world of Eyal, exploring random dungeons, facing challenging battles, and developing characters with your own tailored mix of abilities and powers.&nbsp; With a modern graphical and customisable interface, intuitive mouse control, streamlined mechanics and deep, challenging combat, Tales of Maj&rsquo;Eyal offers engaging roguelike gameplay for the 21st century.<br />
<br />
The continent of Maj&rsquo;Eyal lives in a relative peace.&nbsp; After millennia of war the orcs are extinct, the advanced races have allied together, and the evils of magic have been repressed.&nbsp; But there are still untamed forests, cursed lands, marauding bandits, sealed dungeons of undead, and whispers spread of hidden cults of mages.&nbsp; Some even say the orcs are not all dead, and muster their strength in dark places.<br />
<br />
You are an adventurer, seeking your fortune in the open world, investigating ruins and mysteries in search of riches and powers.&nbsp; Explore a continent still scarred by the terrible Spellblaze from ages past, teeming with wild beasts, monsters, trolls and giants.&nbsp; Delve through dark dungeons infested by armies of undead, demonic monstrosities and hideous horrors.&nbsp; Uncover world changing secrets, from relics of the ancient and all-powerful Sher&rsquo;Tul race to veiled cities of magic and concealed psionic beings.&nbsp; Fight with skill and care against undying necromancers, towering dragons, primeval forces, arcane demons and world-threatening powers.<br />
<br />
Take part in an epic fantasy adventure like no other, engaging both your imagination and your intelligence, demanding thought and attention to succeed.&nbsp; Winner of the Roguelike of the Year award 2010, 2011 and 2012, Tales of Maj&rsquo;Eyal is backed by constant development and an active, friendly community that have together invested over 958 years of total playtime.&nbsp; <strong>250</strong> players are playing right now &ndash; why not join them?</p>Download and have fun!</div>
