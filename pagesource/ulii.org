<p><strong>ULII launches a new website (November 2015)</strong></p>

<p>The Uganda Legal Information Institute (ULII) is one of few resources providing free access to the law of Uganda.</p>

<p>On ULII you can download free:</p>

<ul>
	<li>The most current and complete digital collection of Uganda caselaw from all courts of record in Uganda</li>
	<li>Laws of Uganda, 2000</li>
	<li>A selection of Ugandan legislation as enacted</li>
</ul>
