<h2 class="red title-border-1"><em>Our Levels of Membership are Simply Rewarding!</em></h2>
<p>As a true not-for-profit financial cooperative, the more our member-owners use the services of their credit union, the stronger we become—and the more low-cost services we can provide. That’s the basis of our Membership Rewards program—the more services you use, the more benefits you’ll receive! Whether you are a borrower, saver or both, you will receive extra benefits when you take advantage of the many great products and services at Stanford FCU. It’s easy to reach the highest level of our <span class="red"><a href="https://www.sfcu.org/personal/membership-rewards/">Membership Rewards</a></span> and we can help you switch! You’ll enjoy exclusive benefits like bonus deposit yields and loan rate discounts, unlimited <a href="https://www.sfcu.org/atm-and-branch-locator/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">surcharge-FREE ATMs</a> worldwide and more based on your Membership Rewards level—so why stand for anything less? Make the most out of your banking experience with Membership Rewards.</p>
