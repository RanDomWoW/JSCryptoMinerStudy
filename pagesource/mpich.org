<p style="padding-top: 20px;font-size: 20px;color: #999;line-height: 1.4em"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-103" alt="" src="files/2012/10/rnd100_home.jpg" width="134" height="134" /><span style="font-size: 26px;color: #777;font-weight: bold">MPICH</span> is a <span style="font-size: 22px;color: #6a9275">high performance </span>and <span class="large_green" style="font-size: 22px;color: #6a9275">widely portable</span> implementation of the <strong>Message Passing Interface (MPI)</strong> standard.</p>
<p style="color: #999;line-height: 1.4em">MPICH and its derivatives form the most widely used implementations of MPI in the world. They are used exclusively on nine of the top 10 supercomputers (June 2016 ranking), including the world’s fastest supercomputer: Taihu Light.</p>
