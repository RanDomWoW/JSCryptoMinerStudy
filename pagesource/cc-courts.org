            <div class="alert alert-danger jury-scam">        
               <h3>Public Notice - Jury Scam Alert</h3>
                    <p>
			            Past or prospective jurors are NEVER contacted by phone regarding failure to serve jury duty.  All communication regarding failure to serve jury duty is done via U.S. mail. 
                        No court jury staff or Contra Costa County Sheriff&#39;s staff ask past or prospective jurors to pay a fine or provide financial details including, credit card numbers, bank account, 
                        social security numbers or other personal information. Please do not provide this type of information to anyone.  
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        If you receive a telephone call, from someone identifying himself or herself as a court employee or an employee of the Contra Costa County Sheriff&#39;s Department regarding a 
                        failure to appear for jury service, <strong style="text-decoration:underline">THIS IS A SCAM. DO NOT PROVIDE ANY PERSONAL INFORMATION, OR PAY THEM ANY MONEY!</strong> 
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        If you are contacted, please call the Jury Commissioner&#39;s office at (925) 608-1000.
