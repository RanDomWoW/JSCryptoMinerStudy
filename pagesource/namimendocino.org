<div class="content">
  <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p>NAMI Mendocino County is a grassroots, self-help, support and advocacy organization consisting of families and friends of people living with mental health challenges, clients, professionals and members of the community.</p>
<p>We support people in our community who:</p>
<ul><li>
		Are living with mental health challenges</li>
<li>
		Have a friend or family member living with mental health issues</li>
<li>
		Feel alone and isolated</li>
<li>
		Need information about mental illness, local services and support</li>
</ul><h3>
	Your local NAMI membership will support community resources in Mendocino County.</h3>
<h3>
	<a href="http://www.namimendocino.org/?q=content/join-nami">Join us today!</a></h3>
<p>NAMI's support and public education efforts are focused on offering individuals and the community resources and information about mental illness through a variety of activities, including the following:</p>
<table border="0" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0" style="width:100%"><tbody><tr><td style="vertical-align:top; width:14%">
				<strong>Support</strong></td>
<td>
				<strong>Family Support Groups </strong> provide emotional support to family and friends of people living with mental health issues.  <strong>Check under Community Resourses link for times and locations.</strong><br />
				 </td>
</tr><tr><td style="vertical-align:top">
				<strong>Education</strong></td>
<td>
				Free <strong>Family-to-Family (F2F) and Peer-to-Peer (P2P) </strong>programs focusing on education and information on schizophrenia, bi­polar disorder, major depression, panic and obsessive disorders, biology of the brain, research, medications, treatment, communication &amp; problem solving skills.
<p>				<strong>A Library </strong> of books, pamphlets, audiotapes and videos for loan are available upon request.</p>
<p>				<strong>Speakers </strong> on brain disorders for community groups.<br />
				 </p></td>
</tr><tr><td style="vertical-align:top">
				<strong>Advocacy</strong></td>
<td>
				To put an end to stigma and discrimination and to improve services, including treatment options, housing, education and economic opportunities.</td>
</tr></tbody></table><h3>
