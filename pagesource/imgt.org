<div  class="text-justify">
<p>
<strong>IMGT&reg;, the international ImMunoGeneTics information system&reg; http://www.imgt.org</strong>, is the global reference in
immunogenetics and immunoinformatics, created in 1989 by Marie-Paule Lefranc
(<a href="http://www.umontpellier.fr/">Universit&eacute; de Montpellier</a> and <a href="http://www.cnrs.fr">CNRS</a>).
IMGT&reg; is a high-quality integrated knowledge resource specialized in the immunoglobulins (IG) or antibodies,
T cell receptors (TR), major histocompatibility (MH) of human and other vertebrate species, and in the
immunoglobulin superfamily (IgSF), MH superfamily (MhSF) and related proteins of the immune system (RPI) of vertebrates and invertebrates.
IMGT&reg; provides a common access to sequence, genome and structure Immunogenetics data,
based on the concepts of IMGT-ONTOLOGY and on the IMGT Scientific chart rules.
IMGT&reg; works in close collaboration with
<a href="http://www.ebi.ac.uk/">EBI</a> (Europe),
<a href="http://arsa.ddbj.nig.ac.jp/top-e.html">DDBJ</a> (Japan) and
<a href="http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/gene">NCBI</a> (USA).
IMGT&reg; consists of <span class="sequence">sequence</span> databases, <span class="gene">genome</span> database,
<span class="structure">structure</span> database, and <span class="antibodies">monoclonal antibodies</span> database,
<strong>Web resources</strong> and <strong>interactive tools</strong>.
</p>
<p>
<strong>IMGT founder and director</strong>: <a href="/IMGTinformation/CVMPL.php">Marie-Paule Lefranc</a>
(<a href="mailto:Marie-Paule.Lefranc@igh.cnrs.fr">Marie-Paule.Lefranc@igh.cnrs.fr</a>),
Universit&eacute; de Montpellier, CNRS, <a href="/IMGTinformation/LIGM.php">LIGM</a>,
<a href="http://www.igh.cnrs.fr">IGH</a>, <a href="http://www.biocampus.cnrs.fr/">SFR</a>,
Montpellier (France)
</p>
