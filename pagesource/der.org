						<br>
						<p class="teaser-blurb">Over the past 50 years, thousands of exquisitely painted Maya vases, almost all looted from royal tombs, have flooded into the world's public and private collections. <i>Out of the Maya Tombs</i> enters the world of the vases to explore the royal life and rich mythology of the Maya, as well as the tangled issues involved in the collection and study of Maya art. <a href="http://www.der.org/films/out-of-the-maya-tombs.html" title="Out of the Maya Tombs">Read more <img src="/images/read-more.png" height="12" width="12" class="readmore" alt="read more"></a></p>

						<br>
						<p class="teaser-blurb">In the wake of Robert Mugabe's contentious 2008 presidential win, Zimbabwe convened a bipartisan constitutional committee in an effort to transition the country away from its corrupt authoritarian leadership. Filmed over three years with unprecedented access to the two political rivals overseeing the committee, <i>Democrats</i> is a riveting, firsthand account of a country's fraught first steps towards democracy. <a href="http://www.der.org/films/democrats.html" title="Democrats">Read more <img src="/images/read-more.png" height="12" width="12" class="readmore" alt="read more"></a></p>
