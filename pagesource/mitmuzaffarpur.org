        <div id="introduction">
            <strong>Muzaffarpur Institute of Technology</strong> (MIT), Muzaffarpur is a premier
            institution of eastern India for technical education. It is under administrative
            control of department of science and technology and wholly funded by Govt. of Bihar.
            Established in 1954, MIT is one of the oldest technical institute in India. The
            foundation stone was laid by the first prime minister of India, Pandit Jawaharlal
            Nehru.
            <br />
            <br />
            It is affiliated to
            <a id="ctl00_ContentPlaceHolder1_hl_aryabhatta" href="http://www.akubihar.ac.in/" target="_blank">Aryabhatta Knowlege University, Patna</a>
            &nbsp;and offers undergraduate programs in engineering and pharmacy with postgraduate
            specialisation in Machine design and thermal engineering. The institute also caters
            to the research and development activities of the state of Bihar.
