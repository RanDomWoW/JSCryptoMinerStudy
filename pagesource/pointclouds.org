<h2 class="welcome-box-title">What is it?</h2>
<p>The Point Cloud Library (<strong>PCL</strong>) is a standalone, large scale, <strong>open project</strong> for 2D/3D image and point cloud processing.</p>
<p style="height:10px;"></p>

<p>PCL is released under the terms of the <strong>BSD license</strong>, and thus free for commercial and research use. We are financially supported by a <a href="/about/">consortium of commercial companies</a>, with our own non-profit organization, <a href="http://www.openperception.org">Open Perception</a>. We would also like to thank individual donors and contributors that have been <a href="http://www.openperception.org/support/">helping the project</a>.</p>
