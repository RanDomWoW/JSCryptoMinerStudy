                    <div class="feed-item-title">Traffic Changes for East 101st Street Project</div>
                    <p>
                        Starting March 19, traffic changes will occur for the street rehabilitation project on East 101st Street between South Yale Avenue and South Florence Avenue. Traffic will be limited to one westbound lane between Urbana Avenue and Louisville Avenue until the end of April, when this phase of the project will be completed.

                    <div class="feed-item-title">A Better Way Program Launches</div>
                    <p>
                        Officials from the City of Tulsa, Mental Health Association Oklahoma and the Tulsa Area United Way launched the Better Way Program today giving individuals experiencing homelessness an alternative to panhandling by offering a day’s wages to beautify the community while connecting individuals to services in the community.

                    <div class="feed-item-title">Vision Tulsa: Groundbreaking for TCC Student Center</div>
                    <p>
                        Mayor G.T. Bynum, City Councilor Anna America, and Tulsa Community College officials including President and CEO Leigh B. Goodson, and Board of Regents Chair Paul Cornell held a groundbreaking today for the newest Vision Tulsa project – the Student Success and Career Center at the TCC Southeast Campus, 10300 E. 81st St. The $5.3 million facility is part of the Vision Tulsa package approved by voters in 2016.
