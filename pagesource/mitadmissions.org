<h4 class="tout-follow">Tea with Dava Newman</h4>
<div>
<p>
Enjoy a cup of tea with <a href="http://web.mit.edu/aeroastro/www/people/dnewman/bio.html" target="_blank">Dava Newman</a>, professor of astronautics and engineering systems and former NASA deputy administrator, in <a href="https://youtu.be/6kDyOr4X0W8" target="_blank">this Tea with Teachers video</a>. Hear her talk about being the Head of House of Baker, circumnavigating the globe in a sailboat, and meeting Michelle Obama.</p>

<div class="tout th-all m-all onecol odd t1-t3 mh1-mh2">
<h4 class="tout-afford">Changing the Educational Landscape</h4>
<div>
<p>
When Yazmin Guzman &#39;18 arrived at MIT, she was eager to connect with other Latino students and joined La Casa, a living community that celebrates Latino culture and the Spanish language. <a href="http://news.mit.edu/2018/student-profile-yazmin-guzman-0215" target="_blank">Yazmin explains</a>&nbsp;how the community, as well as working extensively as a mentor, has helped refine her focus toward fixing education inequality and providing opportunities for underresourced youth.</p>
</div>

<div class="tout th-all m-all onecol even t4-t6 mh3-mh4">
<h4 class="tout-apply">Transfer Application for Fall Entry</h4>
<div>
<p>
Although the deadline for the transfer application is March 15, we are lenient with materials sent from schools/evaluators shortly after that time. We ask that you allow seven business days for processing of all materials.</p>
<p>
Please do not resend any materials or follow-up emails to check on the progress without allowing time for processing.</p>
<p><a href="http://mitadmissions.org/apply/transfer/before">More about our transfer process &raquo;</a></p>

<h4 class="tout-visit">Curating the MIT Black Experience</h4>
<img src="http://mitadmissions.org/images/mit-pages/MIT_Black_History_Project.jpg" alt="" class="d4-d5 th-all t1-t3 mh1-mh2 m-all" />
<div class="twocol-content d6-d8 th-clear t4-t6 mh3-mh4 m-clear">
<p>
The MIT Black History Project has <a href="https://www.blackhistory.mit.edu" target="_blank">launched a new website</a> that documents the role of black life at MIT since the Institute opened in 1865. Among hundreds of illustrations and photographs in the digital archive is a never-before-published image of MIT&#39;s first black woman student, <a href="https://www.blackhistory.mit.edu/archive/marie-c-turner-09" target="_blank">Marie C. Turner</a>, who enrolled in 1905 and later became a Boston public school teacher. <a href="http://news.mit.edu/2018/mit-black-history-project-launches-new-website-0221" target="_blank">Learn more about the project</a>.</p>
