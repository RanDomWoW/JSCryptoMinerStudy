<h1 class="contentTitle" id="content">Welcome to mozdev.org</h1>

<p class="invisible"><a title="Skip Content" href="#info-new" accesskey="N" tabindex="7">Skip to What&#8217;s New</a></p>

<!-- system notification section -->

<p id="mozdev-intro">The mozdev.org site provided free project hosting 
for Mozilla applications and extensions.  Developers have many
choices for free project hosting today, but this was the only service 
that was specifically tailored to the needs of the Mozilla
community.  <a href="http://www.mozdev.org/drupal/wiki/WhyMozdev">Read more</a> to find out how mozdev.org compares with
other popular hosting sites.
