        <h2 class="block__title">How Does It Work?</h2>
    
  <div id="node-15526" class="node node-block view-mode-full clearfix">

        
  
  <div class="content">
    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><ol>
<li>Bike tourists and hosts sign up on the site.</li>
<li>The interactive map and other search tools let travelers find hosts on their route.</li>
<li>Travelers send a private message to potential hosts through the site.</li>
<li>Hosts may offer hospitality consisting of a couch, a room, or a place to camp.</li>
</ol>
