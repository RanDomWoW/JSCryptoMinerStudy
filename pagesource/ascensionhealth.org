		<h2>Welcome to Ascension Health</h2>
<p>Like other Catholic healthcare systems, Ascension Health is directed by the Church to care for those most in need. Our Catholic philosophy permeates our national health ministries and our promise to provide Healthcare That Works, Healthcare That is Safe, and Healthcare That Leaves No One Behind.<br /><br />In support of our healing Mission, and driven by compassion and dedication to care for those most in need, Ascension Health has become a leading voice for Catholic healthcare in the United States.<br /><br />We envision a future filled with hope for our national health ministries and those we serve. We are dedicated to spiritually centered holistic care, and are advocates for a compassionate and just society. With your continued support and prayers, we have every reason to envision a vibrant future in Catholic health ministry.</p>	</div>
