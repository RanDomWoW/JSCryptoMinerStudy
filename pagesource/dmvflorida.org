find what you are looking for. Our mission is to make it easy for you to complete your Florida DMV needs online.</p>

<p>Common services provided by the DMV include drivers license renewal, license plate renewal, getting a learners permit, and finding a Florida department of Motor Vehicles approved traffic school.</p>

<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/adi-traffic-school.shtml" title="adi traffic school">ADI Traffic School</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">This new <a href="http://www.123driving.com/adi-class.shtml" title="florida dmv traffic school">online version of ADI (12 hour Florida traffic School)</a> shows how the DMV in Florida is taking advantage of technology to deliver better service to Florida drivers.</p>

<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/traffic-violation-points.shtml">Know the Points before you Pay Your Traffic Violation</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Most traffic violations in Florida will result in points on your driver license. Understand the violation points system and how to avoid getting points.</p>



<br />

<h2><a href="/florida511.shtml">Use the Florida 511 System to know highway conditions before you start driving in Florida</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Florida 511 Traffic updates on the web, by phone call, or text alerts sent to your cell phone tell you whats happening on Florida Roads and Highways so you can take a safe and trouble free route.</p>


<br />

<h2><a href="/car-accident.shtml">What to do if you are in a Car Accident in Florida</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">If you are involved in a car accident in Florida that results in injuries, death, or damage to a vehicle or any other property in excess of $500 you must immediately report the accident to local law enforcement. Learn the proper steps to take
if you are in a car accident.</p>

<br />


<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/ignition-interlock.shtml" title="dui penalties in florida">The Florida Ignition Interlock</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Get convicted of a <a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/florida-dui.shtml" title="drunk driving in florida">DUI in Florida</a> and theres a good chance you'll be forced to have a <a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/ignition-interlock.shtml">high tech breathalyzer</a> installed in your car. You have to blow to start
your car and randomly as you drive.</p>

<br />


<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/new-york-dmv.shtml">DMV Information for New York Licensed Drivers</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">If you have a New York Drivers License and spend time driving in Florida you can find many New York DMV services online and have a new <a href="http://www.nydefensivedrivingcourse.org/">NY Defensiving Driving Course option</a> to save on your NY auto insurance and reduce points
on your driving record.</p>


<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/florida-dmv-driving-record.shtml" title="dmv driving record">Florida DMV Driving Records</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Can't remember the last time you got a speeding ticket? Get your <a href="http://www.hsmv.state.fl.us/ddl/dlfaqson.html#record" title="dmv driving records">Florida driving record by email</a>.</p>

<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/learner-permit1.shtml" title="learners permit">Learner Permit Questions &amp; Answers</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Have a question about getting your <a href="http://www.hsmv.state.fl.us/ddl/teendriv.html#learner" title="florida learner permit">learner permit</a>? Find answers about the DMV test here.</p>

<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/clearing-a-ticket.shtml" title="outstanding traffic ticket">Clearing Up an Outstanding Traffic Ticket</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">If you have <a href="http://www.hsmv.state.fl.us/ddl/faqsuspend.html#1" title="suspended drivers license">a traffic ticket that is overdue</a> clear it up with the DMV as soon as possible and get your drivers license back. For a recent ticket <a href="http://www.123driving.com/traffic_school_select.shtml" title="driver improvement">consider taking a traffic school class and avoid negative impact on your drivers license</a> and auto insurance.</p>

<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/cell-phone-distractions.shtml" title="florida drivers distracted">Tips from The Florida Highway Patrol</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Distracted drivers are a leading cause of accidents in Florida so <a href="http://www.fhp.state.fl.us/" title="florida highway patrol">the FHP</a> shares tips if you must talk on your cell phone while driving.</p>

<br />

<h2><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/florida-business.shtml" title="florida incorporation">How to Incorporate in Florida</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">Starting a <a href="http://www.sunbiz.org/" title="florida department of state">new business in Florida</a>? Read our guide to incorporating online and descriptions of the different corporation types.</p>

<br />

<h2 class="nobr">Chose Wisely if You Get a Traffic Ticket</h2>
<p class="nobr">Completing a <a href="http://www.123driving.com/" title="florida approved driver improvement">Traffic School course</a> (also known as defensive driving or driver improvement) can be done from home online or on video. In most cases, completing a traffic school course will result in no points on your drivers license and your car insurance cannot go up.</p>

<br />


<h2 class="nobr"><a href="http://www.dmvflorida.org/personal-injury-lawyers.shtml" title="personal injury lawyers">What to do if You are Injured in a Car Accident</a></h2>
<p class="nobr">If you are injured in a car accident and feel it was the result of negligence you may <a href="http://www.aftl.org/" title="florida legal advice">want to get legal advice</a>. The Florida DMV does not recommend attorneys but we have provided some resources to assist you.</p>
