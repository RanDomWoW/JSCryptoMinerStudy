  	<h2 class="pageSubTitle hidden-xs">
<div id="HTMLBlock3832" class="HTMLBlock">
Experience the MAIN ST. &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Fort Worth Arts Festival</div></h2>
  	<hr>
  	<div class="highlights col-md-6">
	  	<p><strong>Mark your calendars now for April 19-22, 2018 </strong>as we celebrate one of the top <strong><span style="color: #5c53a6;">Downtowns </span></strong>in the country along a <strong><span style="color: #fdce01;">magnificent mile of art, music and food</span></strong>. Connect with us on your mobile device with our <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/main-st-fort-worth-arts-festival/id361272737?mt=8">iPhone</a> and <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=enilon.mainstreet">Android</a> apps!</p>
<p>We are located in <strong><span style="color: #ff000f;">Downtown Fort Worth (76102),</span></strong> between Weatherford Street on the north, 9th Street on the south, Commerce Street on the east and Houston Street to the west. 18 blocks of food, fun, music and...art!</p>  	</div>
  	<div class="highlights col-md-6">
	  	<p>The <strong><a href="http://www.mainstreetartsfest.org/experience-main-st./the-art/">2018 Artist's Gallery</a></strong><span style="color: #f10de8;"><strong><a href="http://www.mainstreetartsfest.org/experience-main-st./the-art/">,</a></strong></span> showcasing our invited artists, juried in from over 1200 applicants, is now live! Please join us in welcoming new sponsors <strong><span style="color: #5c53a6;">Michelob Ultra and Chloe Wine Collection.</span></strong></p>
<p>The <strong><span style="color: #ff000f;"><a href="http://www.mainstreetartsfest.org/experience-main-st./music-on-main/">2018 Entertainment Schedule</a></span></strong> has been posted, showcasing hundreds of entertainers throughout Downtown, FREE! We'll see YOU Downtown at <span style="color: #0b14f3;"><strong>Texas' largest, FREE, Music and Arts festival!</strong></span></p>  	</div>

<p><span>Often perceived as paintings, Dolan's art pieces are actually composed of hundreds of pieces of paper, cut by my own hands, and then affixed to a wood panel. All of the paper used for these collages comes from abandoned spaces--old factories, old farmhouses, warehouses, and materials found on the streets, and nothing newer than 1960. Once he has gathered up a pile of vintage materials, he sketches out an image on a board, then cuts and glues all of the papers to the surface. </span></p>
<p>His images often tell a story, and this year's commissioned poster, available now online either signed or unsigned, was created for MAIN ST. and is printed on Mohawk Options cover paper stock courtesy of Olmsted Kirk Paper Company and printed by Cockrell Enovation. The 20" wide x 28" high poster was designed by Pinkerton Design, and is now available for shipment packed in a sturdy packing tube.</p></div></div><div class="ccm-spacer"></div></div></div></div>
