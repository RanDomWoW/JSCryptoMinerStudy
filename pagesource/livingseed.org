<h4>Deliberate Choices Jesus Made – 2</h4>
<h4><a href="http://livingseed.org/the-form-of-a-servant/">. . . THE FORM OF A SERVANT</a></h4>
<p><em>“Who, being in the form of God, did not consider it robbery to be equal with God, but made Himself of no reputation, <strong>taking the form of a bondservant</strong>, and coming in the likeness of men&#8221;. </em><em>Phil 2:6-7</em></p>
<p>Everyone grows up to become a ‘master’. Experience, expertise and exposures tend to make a man a master. Exploits done for the Lord will automatically place a status on your life. Years of service, years of ministry, years of revelation from God seek to make a ‘master’ out of you. <strong>‘Masters’</strong> are men who direct but for Jesus, this was not so. He took upon Himself the form of a servant! This again was His personal choice.</p>
<p>No one imposed it on Him. He took it upon Himself. This is the crux of the matter. If you desire to remain a master it is your right. Years of service have probably brought it on you; exposure, experience, expertise and exploits have made and carved out such a seat for you. But you can lay it aside to continually tie around yourself the towel of service . . .</p>
