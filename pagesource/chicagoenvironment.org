<p>Where do you go when you care about the natural world and want to get more involved? Maybe you already recycle but want to do more. 
Or perhaps you're a teacher and you want your students to understand—and help protect—the natural resources around them. Possibly you work for 
an environmental organization and want to reach other organizations with the same goals.</p>

<p>The <strong>Chicagoland Environmental Network (CEN)</strong>, a group of more than 200 environmental and natural resource organizations, 
<strong>can get you connected</strong>.</p>

<p>Who uses CEN? <strong>People just like you.</strong> CEN is a resource for anyone interested in nature or conservation-related activities 
in northeastern Illinois and surrounding areas. CEN organizations are involved in important work, including habitat restoration; wetland, 
prairie, and watershed projects; urban gardening; energy conservation; composting, wildlife conservation; and recycling.

<p>Click a tab above to search the CEN database for volunteer opportunities, jobs and internships, family activities, or to learn more about CEN organizations.</p>
