            <div class="gutter-nested"><div class="box2 feature"><h3>Featured partner</h3><h4>Soci&#xE9;t&#xE9; Pan Africaine de Statistiques</h4>
  <p>Also known as the Statistics and Probability African Society, the SPAS is headquartered in Saint-Louis, Senegal and draws its members from across Africa and the diaspora. <a href="/euclid.as">Afrika Statistika</a> is the society's official journal and publishes applied and theoretical work on probability, statistics, operational research, econometrics and related topics. The journal launched in 2005, and every issue is hosted on Project Euclid and available to libraries through the Euclid Prime collection.</p>
