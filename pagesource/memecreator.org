<font class="c3">What are meme's?</font>
The term 'meme' gained popularity in 2012, even making it to the top 10 most searched words of the year by Merriam Webster - America's leading dictionary publisher. Meme Creator launched before the buzz in early 2011 and has aided netizens in creating over 3 million memes to date. It has been featured in many major online publications including About.com's guide to web trends on making memes.
<br><br>
Meme's by definition are an element of a culture or system of behavior that may be considered to be passed from one individual to another by non-genetic means, especially imitation.
According to google meme are; a humorous image, video, piece of text, etc. that is copied (often with slight variations) and spread rapidly by Internet users.
<br><br>
It was originally Britian's Richard Dawkins who coined the term 'meme' in his 1976 book "The Selfish Gene". The definition still remains true today though most people associate memes with thoughts on popular culture or current events. Still the point of making a meme on Meme Creator is to share it so it can 'go viral' and spread, which is most easily done through social media.
<br><br>
At the end of the day, meme's created on Meme Creator are meant for humor and fun. Make, share and enjoy the meme's made with our generator but remember, it's the internet, don't take it too seriously. One last note, memes meant for bullying or harassment aren't tolerated here. If someone is using a meme on our site to bully you, get in touch with us asap so we can address it. Take care, have fun, and enjoy the memes!
