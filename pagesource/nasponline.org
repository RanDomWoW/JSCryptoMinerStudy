		<h3 class="content-box-title">The NASP Practice Model</h3>
    
<div class="margin-large formatted ">
	<p><img alt="" class="img-right" src="Images/content-practice-model.jpg">The <a href="https://www.nasponline.org/Documents/Standards and Certification/Standards/2_PracticeModel.pdf" target="_blank">NASP <em>Model for Comprehensive and Integrated School Psychological Services</em></a>&nbsp;(PDF), also known as the <strong>NASP Practice Model</strong>, represents NASP's official policy regarding the delivery of school psychological services. NASP has developed <a href="/standards-and-certification/nasp-practice-model/nasp-practice-model-implementation-guide">this implementation guide</a> as a resource to help school psychologists through the process of advocating for and implementing the model in their school or district.</p>
