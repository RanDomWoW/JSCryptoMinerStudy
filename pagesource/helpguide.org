<h2 style="clear:both;">Dedicated to Morgan Leslie Segal</h2>
<div class="box info">
<p>HelpGuide is dedicated to <a href="/misc/morgan-segal.htm">Morgan Leslie Segal</a>. For six years, she
grappled with a condition that started as low self-esteem and worsened into major depression. She
stuck with a single therapeutic mode and her prescribed medications even though the combination just
made her worse. Finally, in desperation, she committed suicide just after her 29th birthday.</p>
<p>Morgan loved the Internet. If she had access to the wide range of unbiased, motivating resources and
self-help tools HelpGuide now provides, we believe her suicide could have been prevented.</p>
