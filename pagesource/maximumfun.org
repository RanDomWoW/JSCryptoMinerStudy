<p><center><img src="http://www.maximumfun.org/images/WrinklePic.jpg" /></center><center><i>A Wrinkle in Time</i></center></p>
<p>
We've got a very special episode this week as Alonso Duralde takes over hosting duties. He's joined by film critic for Tribune News Service and the LA Times, Katie Walsh, Vidiots Executive Director/Board Chair, Maggie Mackay, and our dear friend, producer and film festival programmer, Drea Clark. They break down the much discussed <i>A Wrinkle in Time</i>. They address the blowback the film has received, what they really liked about it, and the revolutionary nature of having a protagonist that is a woman of color. They also discuss kids movies in general and some of their favorites. In fact, a fight nearly breaks out over whether <i>The NeverEnding Story</i> is actually a good movie. PLUS, April Wolfe calls in from South by Southwest to give everyone an update.
</p>

<p>In news, psychologist Jordan Peterson hates <a href="http://time.com/5176537/jordan-peterson-frozen-movie-disney/"><i>Frozen</i></a>, Tommy Wiseau auditions for <a href="http://www.indiewire.com/2018/03/tommy-wiseau-audition-the-joker-video-1201938455/">The Joker</a>, production companies start adopting <a href="http://www.nme.com/news/film/matt-damon-ben-affleck-inclusion-riders-2261805">inclusion riders</a>, and the man who stole Frances McDormand's Oscar pleads <a href="http://www.nme.com/news/film/man-allegedly-stole-frances-mcdormands-oscar-says-took-pleads-not-guilty-2257870">not guilty</a>.</p>
<p>Weekly Movie Recommendations:</p>
<p>Alonso - <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_3E9MGOK4LY">Ramen Heads</a><br />
Drea - <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=auvqupS3fWY">No Light and No Land Anywhere</a><br />
Katie - <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=y1SpWZm1PLc">American Honey</a><br />
Maggie - <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OZzb_6LtMjY">After Hours</a></p>
<p>PLUS, we have two Spotify playlists. One is for <a href="https://open.spotify.com/user/127865548/playlist/3QrILKicobtwL05g2GCb0m">Movie Soundtracks</a> and the other is for <a href="https://open.spotify.com/user/127865548/playlist/08AduW0guGte9jnfTlANAz">Movie Scores</a>. Check 'em out!</p>
