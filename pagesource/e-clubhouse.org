				<h2><strong>Create a Free Website for Your Club</strong></h2>

<p>If you&rsquo;re looking for a free and easy way to create a website for your Lions Club, the e-Clubhouse is for you!</p>

<p>LionNet is proud to provide this resource to help Lions Clubs around the world build a useful, professional looking website. Designed with fill-in-the blank fields for even the least internet savvy, the e-Clubhouse allows you to:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Build and maintain a website for your club using a simple set of tools.</li>
	<li>Keep your members &ndash; and community &ndash; up-to-date about the projects the club is working on.</li>
	<li>Promote your club&rsquo;s activities and recruit new members.</li>
</ul>

<p>You can choose between two predesigned Website templates that incorporate the Lions Clubs International brand. Both templates include links to information on the Lions Clubs International Website, including the Lions News Network, Lions Learning Center and more. &nbsp;</p>

<h2>e-Clubhouse Features</h2>

<p>Each e-Clubhouse features:</p>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Club Home Page</strong>: Feature a photo of your club members participating in a project or club activity and provide a brief history of your club.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Calendar of Events</strong>: Use the calendar to keep everyone informed of important dates.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Club Projects</strong>: Provide information on your projects and let your community know how they can support your projects.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Photo Gallery</strong>: Showcase your club&#39;s activities by posting photos of recent projects.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li><strong>Contact Page</strong>: Post key contact information for people in your community to call so they can learn more about your club and get involved.</li>
</ul>

<ul>
	<li>Up to five additional pages for your club&#39;s special functions or news.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Member-Only Features</h2>

<p>The member-only section provides a way for your members to communicate important club news, encourage attendance to meetings and recognize member dedication.&nbsp;</p>

<ul>
	<li>Member Directory</li>
	<li>Message Boards</li>
	<li>E-vites</li>
	<li>Polls</li>
	<li>Member and Group Recognition page</li>
	<li>Newsletter template</li>
	<li>Group email</li>
</ul>

<h2>Get Started Today</h2>

<p><a href="http://e-clubhouse.org/application.php">Submit an application</a> to create a Website for your Lions club today.</p>
