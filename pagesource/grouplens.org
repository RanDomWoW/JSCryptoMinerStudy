<h2 class="top">Featured Research</h2>
<p>We publish research articles in conferences and journals primarily in the field of computer science, but also in other fields including psychology, sociology, and medicine. See our <a href="/blog/">blog</a> for research highlights and our <a href="/publications/">publications</a> page for a comprehensive view of our research contributions. Here are excerpts from recent articles:</p>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-sm-6"><h4>Love Online in Saudi Arabia &#8230; It’s Complicated</h4>
<p>Finding love online is complicated and usually comes with a side of  social stigma. Sure, true love might exist on the inter-webs, but you also might struggle with skeptical family and friends who believe otherwise. Now just imagine replicating the scenario in the conservative culture of Saudi Arabia. Our new DIS paper shows just how complicated things become.<br />
First, dating is not allowed&#8230;or at least not the way you would imagine a “date” to be. It usually involves parents being present, and we all know how awkward that can get. If you are lucky, you might get some private time together, but it would be in the house of your would-be in laws. <a href="https://grouplens.org/blog/love-online-in-saudi-arabia-its-complicated/">Read more.</a></p>
</div>
<div class="col-sm-6"><h4>Different Strokes for Different Folks: The Value of Personality Type in Recommender Systems and Social Computing</h4>
<p><img width="476" height="327" src="https://grouplens.org/site-content/uploads/personality.png" class="img-responsive gl-featured-image wp-post-image" alt="">Did you know that your personality type can be used to predict your behavior on an online recommender site (how long you stay, what you do, whether and how much you are likely to rate)  and even what to recommend to you? That’s what we found in our latest research using the MovieLens recommender system and the Big Five Personality scale for modeling user personality. <a href="https://grouplens.org/blog/different-strokes-for-different-folks-the-value-of-personality-type-in-recommender-systems-and-social-computing/">Read more.</a></p>
</div>
</div>
</section>
<section class="gl-front-section">
<h2>Featured Projects</h2>
<p>We build and study real systems, going back to the release of <a href="https://movielens.org">MovieLens</a> in 1997. See our <a href="/projects/">projects</a> page for a full list of active projects; see below for some featured projects.</p>
<div class="break"></div>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-sm-4">
<div><a href="https://movielens.org"><br />
<img class="img-responsive" src="//grouplens.org/site-content/uploads/ml-logo.png" alt="MovieLens" /><br />
</a></div>
<p class="spaced">MovieLens is a web site that helps people find movies to watch. It has hundreds of thousands of registered users. We conduct online field experiments in MovieLens in the areas of automated content recommendation, recommendation interfaces, tagging-based recommenders and interfaces, member-maintained databases, and intelligent user interface design.</p>
</div>
<div class="col-sm-4">
<div><a href="http://cyclopath.org"><br />
<img class="img-responsive" src="/assets/img/projects/cyclopath.png" alt="Cyclopath" /><br />
</a></div>
<p class="spaced">Find bike routes that match the way you ride. Share your cycling knowledge with the community. Cyclopath is a geowiki: an editable map where anyone can share notes about roads and trails, enter tags about special locations, and fix map problems &#8211; like missing trails. Hundreds of Twin Cities cyclists are already doing this, making Cyclopath the most comprehensive and up-to-date bicycle information resource in the world.</p>
</div>
<div class="col-sm-4">
<div><a href="http://lenskit.org/"><br />
<img class="img-responsive" src="/assets/img/projects/lenskit.png" alt="LensKit" /><br />
</a></div>
<p class="spaced">LensKit is an open source toolkit for building, researching, and studying recommender systems. Do you need a recommender for your next project? LensKit provides high-quality implementations of well-regarded collaborative filtering algorithms and is designed for integration into web applications and other similarly complex environments.</p>
</div>
</div>
</section>
