			<h1 style="font-size:20px; line-height:24px;"><a href="http://www.pnhp.org/nhi"><img src="/sites/default/files/NHI_final.jpg" alt="#BeyondACA" title="#BeyondACA" width="300" height="300" /></a></h1>
<p>On May 5, 2016, an esteemed group of physicians unveiled a detailed plan for single-payer health care in the U.S. To <strong>read the proposal</strong>, please visit <a href="http://www.pnhp.org/nhi">pnhp.org/nhi</a>. To read and view <strong>media coverage</strong> of the proposal, <a href="http://www.pnhp.org/nhimedia">click here</a>. To browse supplemental materials related to the proposal, <a href="http://www.pnhp.org/beyondaca">click here</a>.</p>
<hr />
<h1 style="font-size:20px; line-height:24px;"><a href="http://www.pnhp.org/MedicareForAll"><img src="/sites/default/files/senate.jpg" alt="Sanders Bill" title="Sanders Bill" width="300" height="224" /></a></h1>
<p>PNHP welcomes the introduction of Sen. Sanders' single-payer legislation as a landmark moment in the fight for single payer. To <strong>learn more about the Medicare For All Act</strong> of 2017, including PNHP's analysis of how the bill could be strengthened, visit <a href="http://www.pnhp.org/MedicareForAll">pnhp.org/MedicareForAll</a>.</p>
<hr />
<h1><a href="http://www.pnhp.org/gop">The conservative case for single payer</a></h1>
<h1 style="font-size:20px; line-height:24px;"><a href="http://www.pnhp.org/gop"><img src="/sites/default/files/GOP_Graphic_0.png" alt="HR676_GOP" title="HR676_GOP" width="300" height="158" /></a></h1>
<p>As our health care crisis deepens, Americans are demanding real solutions that <em>expand</em> coverage and <em>reduce</em> cost-sharing. <strong>Many conservatives recognize the need for fundamental reform</strong> and have voiced support for improved Medicare for all. For more information on the conservative case for single payer, <a href="http://www.pnhp.org/gop">click here</a>.</p>
