<div class="uk-width-1-1"><div class="uk-panel uk-panel-box _sidebox_grey"><h3 class="uk-panel-title">Summer 2018 Internship Opportunities with San Francisco's Earthquake Safety Implementation Program</h3>
	<p>Internships with San Francisco's Earthquake Safety Implementation Program are available for Summer 2018. The City and County of San Francisco's <em>Community Action Plan for Seismic Safety</em> study, completed in 2010, provided an analysis of anticipated impacts that likely scenario earthquakes might have on the City, including physical, social, cultural, economic and other factors. That study led to specific recommended actions to reduce those impacts. San Francisco leaders are committed to implementing those recommendations. Our program supports that implementation. <a href="/summer-2018-internship" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Learn more.</a></p></div></div>

<div class="uk-width-1-1"><div class="uk-panel uk-panel-box _sidebox_grey"><h3 class="uk-panel-title">What's New?</h3>
	<p><br /><strong>What's New?</strong> The ATC Design Loads websites allow the user to find site-specific basic <a href="http://windspeed.atcouncil.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">wind speeds</a> and <a href="http://snowload.atcouncil.org/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ground snow loads</a> used in the standard published by the American Society of Civil Engineers in 2010. These websites were developed with the financial help of the <a href="/index.php/about-atc/atc-endowment-fund" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">ATC Endowment Fund</a> and is free of charge to users.</p>
<p><a href="/products"><img src="/images/stories/product_hilite2.png" alt="product hilite" width="537" height="92" border="0" vspace="5" /></a></p>
<p><strong><a href="https://store.atcouncil.org/" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">Available Products.</a> </strong>The library of completed ATC products includes more than 100 publications on key issues in structural, earthquake, wind, and coastal engineering.  Some are available as free downloads and others can be purchased through ATC’s newly updated <a href="https://store.atcouncil.org/" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">online store</a>.   <a href="/products" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">More…</a></p>
<p><strong><a href="/projects/projects-active" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">Projects.</a></strong> With funding from various government agencies and the <a href="/index.php/about-atc/atc-endowment-fund" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">ATC Endowment Fund</a>, ATC is actively engaged in a broad range of funded technical projects to mitigate the effects of natural and other hazards on the built environment.</p>
<p><strong><a href="/events" target="_blank" rel="alternate noopener noreferrer">Events.</a></strong> Periodically ATC conducts seminars, conferences, workshops, webinars, and other meetings to document and advance the state of structural engineering practice and to identify research needs.  Registration for these events can be made through the “Events” section of this website.  ATC also has the ability to provide an instructor to conduct your <a href="/products/training-info1/59-on-demand-training" rel="alternate">ATC-20 and ATC-45 in-person trainings</a>, and information about these options is available under On Demand Training in the Product menu above.</p></div></div>
