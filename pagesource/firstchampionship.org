<h1>Welcome to the <em>FIRST<sup>®</sup></em> Championship</h1>
</div>
<p>Prepare to be inspired as you watch tens of thousands of students compete on the world stage at the <em>FIRST</em><sup>®</sup> Championship presented by Qualcomm<sup>®</sup> Incorporated. With all the heart-pounding thrills and team spirit of a professional sporting event, <em>FIRST</em> uses robotics as the backbone for the “only sport where every kid can go pro.” Guided by mentors and STEM professionals, and supported by <em>FIRST </em>Sponsors, <em>FIRST</em> teams inspire kids in grades K-12 to explore the innovation process and build STEM and digital-literacy skills. Watch the exciting competitions and visit teams in the “pits.” We guarantee you will leave impressed.</p>
