            <h1>What is Browsershots?</h1>
            <p>
                Browsershots makes screenshots of your web design in different operating systems and browsers.
                It is a free open-source online web application providing developers a convenient way to test their website's browser compatibility in one place.
                When you submit your web address, it will be added to the job queue.
                A number of distributed computers will open your website in their browser.
                Then they will make screenshots and upload them to our central dedicated server for your review.
