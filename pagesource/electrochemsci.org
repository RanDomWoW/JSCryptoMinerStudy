<h2>International Journal of Electrochemical Science</h2>
<p><i>International Journal of Electrochemical Science</i> (ISSN 1452-3981) is international peer-reviewed and open access journal that publishes original research articles, short communications as well as review articles in the field of electrochemistry. Publication Frequency: 12 issues per year. The abbreviated title is <i><b>Int. J. Electrochem. Sci.</b></i></p>
    <p>The publisher of the journal is <a href="http://www.electrochemsci.org/ESG.htm">ESG</a>.</p>
<p><b>Impact factor (Thomson Reuters): </b>1.469 (2016)</p>
