                <h2 class="color-white font-volkhov">
                    <a href="https://youtu.be/EdPFWZOpxdQ" data-lightbox="" style="color: #c2dfac;">What is a Charter School? <i class="fa fa-film color-white"></i></a>
                </h2>
                <p class="color-white text-larger">
                    Charter schools are free independent public schools with rigorous curriculum programs and unique educational approaches. In exchange for operational freedom and flexibility, charter schools are subject to higher levels of accountability
                    than traditional public schools. Charter schools, which are tuition-free and open to all students, offer quality and choice in the public education system.
