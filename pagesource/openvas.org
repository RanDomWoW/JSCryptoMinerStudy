    <h2>The world's most advanced Open Source vulnerability scanner and manager</h2>
    <p>
    OpenVAS is a framework of several services and tools offering a
    comprehensive and powerful vulnerability scanning and vulnerability
    management solution.
    The framework is part of
    <a href="http://www.greenbone.net/en/">Greenbone Networks</a>'
    commercial vulnerability management solution from which developments are
    contributed to the Open Source community since 2009.
    </p>
</div>

<div id="full">

Learn what OpenVAS is and read more about the features of our solution!
</p>
<a href="about.html">About OpenVAS »</a>
</div>

<div class="column2">
<h4>Try out OpenVAS</h4>

<p>
<a href="vm.html">Try out OpenVAS in a Virtual Machine »</a>

<div class="column3">
<h4>Join the community</h4>
<p>OpenVAS is Free Software. Join the community!
We recommend subscribing to the
<a href="http://lists.wald.intevation.org/mailman/listinfo/openvas-announce"
>OpenVAS-Announcement mailing list</a>
to be automatically informed about new releases and other
important OpenVAS news.
</p>
<p>
<a href="online-chat.html">Join the Online Chat »</a>
</p>
</div>
<div class="column1">
<h4>Reliable and Sustainable</h4>
<p>
The continuously growing customer base of Greenbone guarantees
the permanent maintenance and extension of the OpenVAS framework.
</p>

<p>
Dedicated developers and users are brought together
with the open and transparent approach and thus contribute
significantly to quality and innovation.
</p>
</div>

<div class="column2">
<h4>Supporters</h4>
<p>
The German <a href="http://www.bsi.de">Federal Office for Information Security (BSI)</a> supported various features of the
OpenVAS software framework as well as various network vulnerability tests.
</p>
<p>
<a href="http://www.dfn-cert.de/en.html">DFN-CERT</a> contributes their advisories to the OpenVAS Security Feed.
</p>

<p align="center">
<a href="http://www.bsi.de"><img border="0" src="img/bsi-logo_150x100.png"/></a>
<a href="http://www.dfn-cert.de/en.html"><img border="0" src="img/dfn-cert.png"/></a>
</p>

</div>
<div class="column3">
    <h4>Activity</h4>
<p>
Improvements and extension to the framework are contributed every single day.
Due the open approach the Greenbone development team also receives support
from various users and experts:
penetration testers, power users, security researchers, academia etc.
</p>
<p>
Browse the <a href="https://www.openhub.net/p/openvas">development activity</a> on Open HUB.
