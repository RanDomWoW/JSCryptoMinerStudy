The legal details on standing, jurisdiction, etc. are all very interesting (follow <a href="https://twitter.com/ColumbiaClimate">@ColumbiaClimate</a> for those details), but somewhat uniquely, the judge (William Alsup) has asked for a tutorial on climate science (2 hours of evidence from the plaintiffs and the defendents).
