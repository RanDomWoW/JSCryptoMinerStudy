<h2>KCKPS Parents, Students &amp; the Community are Encouraged to Share What's Important for the District at Listening Tours</h2>
<p style="color: blue;"><em>Posted Thursday, March 1, 2018</em></p>
<p><img src="/images/board/images/newboard2018.jpg" alt="2018 Board of Education Photo" width="152" height="100" style="margin: 5px; float: right;" />The Kansas City, Kansas Board of Education is presenting “Listening Tours” for the school district’s parents, students and the community to share what’s important to them and their families regarding the Kansas City, Kansas Public Schools (KCKPS).</p>
<p>The Listening Tours will give the KCK Board of Education a chance to hear first-hand about these important opportunities which could help inform future board priorities. It will also give parents, students and the community a chance to meet the new KCK Board.&nbsp;<a href="/index.php/news-releases/900-kckps-parents-students-the-community-are-encouraged-to-share-what-s-important-for-the-district-at-listening-tours">Read More</a></p>

<h2>Notice - Time and Place of Special Meeting - March 26, 2018</h2>
<p style="color: blue;"><em>Posted Wednesday, March 14, 2018</em></p>
<p>A special meeting of the Kansas City, Kansas Board of Education is hereby called for Monday, March 26, 2018, at 5:00 p.m., in the third floor Board room of the KCKPS Central Office and Training Center, 2010N. 59th Street, Kansas City, Kansas.&nbsp;</p>
<p>In addition to regular business itemized in the published agenda, the Board shall continue work on the 2018-2019 budget with an overview and planning for the public libraries.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>Notice - Time and Place of Special Meeting - March 17, 2018</h2>
<p style="color: blue;"><em>Posted Wednesday, March 14, 2018</em></p>
<p>A special meeting of the Kansas City, Kansas Board of Education is hereby called for Saturday, March 17, 2018, at 11:00 a.m. in the third floor Board room at the KCKPS Central Office and Training Center, 2010 N. 59th Street, Kansas City, Kansas.</p>
<p>The Board shall meet to interview and select a firm or individual to conduct a nationwide search for a superintendent of schools for the Kansas City, Kansas Public School District.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><img src="/images/home_slider_03-18/springbreakslide.png" alt="Spring Break - No School March 9-16" width="672" height="450" /></p>
<h2>Notice - Time and Place of Special Meeting - March 10, 2018</h2>
<p style="color: blue;"><em>Posted Wednesday, March 7, 2018</em></p>
<p>A special meeting of the Kansas City, Kansas Board of Education is hereby called for Saturday, March 10, 2018, at 3:00 p.m. at the South Branch Library, 3104 Strong Avenue, Kansas City, Kansas. The Board shall meet to review responses to the Request for Information/Proposal for the Superintendent Search and select firms or individuals to be interviewed.&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2>KCKPS Extends School Day for High School Students</h2>
<p style="color: blue;"><em>Posted Wednesday, March 7, 2018</em></p>
<p>The four days the district closed school because of winter weather conditions have created a problem for high schools in Kansas City, Kansas in terms of meeting the number of hours high schools are required to be in session during the school year. According to the Kansas Department of Education, state law requires not less than 1,116 hours of school for students in K-11, and 1,086 for high school seniors.&nbsp;<a href="/index.php/news-releases/905-kckps-extends-school-day-for-high-school-students">Read More</a></p>
<p></p>

<p><img src="/images/schools/images/sumner-2002.jpg" alt="Sumner Academy photo" width="106" height="100" style="margin: 5px; float: right;" />The Sumner Admissions Appeal Application is open from March 5th to March 16th.</p>

<p><img src="/images/home_slider_03-18/gloriawillisrender.jpg" alt="Architectural Rendering of Gloria Willis Middle School" width="132" height="100" style="margin: 5px; float: right;" />The Kansas City, Kansas Public Schools will host three groundbreaking events on Thursday, March 8, to celebrate the kick-off to the construction of a new middle school, as well as, security upgrades and classroom additions at two of the school district’s elementary locations.&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="/index.php/news-releases/908-kckps-to-host-groundbreaking-events-for-school-improvements">Read More</a></p>
