<img class="img" src="/static/img/noticeboard_main.jpg" srcset="/static/img/noticeboard_main@2x.jpg 2x, /static/img/noticeboard_main@1.5x.jpg 1.5x" style="width: 309px; height: 232px;" />
<div class="description">
<h2>A community knowledge commons</h2>
<p>
What if there was somewhere you could go to learn all about where you live? A place to learn about your <a href="/oakland/Neighborhoods" target="_blank">neighborhoods</a>, about your local <a href="/santacruz/Parks_and_Open_Spaces" target="_blank">parks</a>, about being a <a href="/davis/Parenting" target="_blank">parent in your city</a>, about <a href="/tags/history" target="_blank">history</a>, <a href="/detroit/Detroit_Department_of_Transportation_%28DDOT%29" target="_blank">politics</a> &ndash; anything and everything useful and beautiful about your community. With LocalWiki, everybody has a way to share and learn about where they live.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="item">
<div>
<img class="img" src="/static/img/storytelling.jpg" srcset="/static/img/storytelling@2x.jpg 2x, /static/img/storytelling@1.5x.jpg 1.5x" style="width: 309px; height: 232px;" />
<div class="description">
<h2>A collaborative storytelling tool</h2>
<p>
Whether it's knowing about <a href="/sf/Green_Hairstreak_Butterfly_Corridor" target="_blank">butterfly corridors</a> or the history of a <a href="/islavista/Coal_Oil_Point" target="_blank">local beach</a>, everyone holds a piece of the puzzle of what it's like to live in their community. And unlike a news site or a blog, anyone can add and modify LocalWiki pages. LocalWiki lets you and your neighbors tell the story of your community, together.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="item">
<div>
<img class="img" src="/static/img/global_effort.jpg" srcset="/static/img/global_effort@2x.jpg 2x, /static/img/global_effort@1.5x.jpg 1.5x" style="width: 309px; height: 232px;" />
<div class="description">
<h2>A global effort</h2>
<p>
From <a href="https://ja.localwiki.org/sapporo/" target="_blank">Sapporo, Japan</a> to <a href="/oakland/" target="_blank">Oakland, California</a>, communities across the world use LocalWiki to share their local knowledge. Learn about the issues that affect nearby communities, and communities halfway across the globe. There is a whole world to discover!
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="item">
<div>
<img class="img noborder" src="/static/img/cc_main_page.png" srcset="/static/img/cc_main_page@2x.png 2x, /static/img/cc_main_page@1.5x.png 1.5x" style="width: 309px; height: 232px;" />
<div class="description">
<h2>Free, open and non-profit</h2>
<p>
Like a park or a public library, LocalWiki is a public good. LocalWiki is free for anyone to <a href="/main/Copyrights" target="_blank">read, copy and reuse for any purpose</a>, and the backend code powering LocalWiki is open-source. We do not run any advertisements, and we are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. You can <a href="https://www.patreon.com/localwiki" target="_blank">donate to support LocalWiki here</a>.
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="item">
<div>
<img class="img" src="/static/img/api_for_city_main.jpg" srcset="/static/img/api_for_city_main@2x.jpg 2x, /static/img/api_for_city_main@1.5x.jpg 1.5x" style="width: 309px; height: 205px;" />
<div class="description">
<h2>An API for your city</h2>
<p>
LocalWiki represents a free, universally-accessible collection of the world's local knowledge upon which we hope countless civic and locally-relevant applications will be built. Our <a href="/api/" target="_blank">geospatially-aware, read-write API</a> makes it easy to integrate LocalWiki with your application, or to import useful local data from elsewhere!
</p>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<div class="item">
<div>
<img class="img" src="/static/img/page_edit_screenshot.jpg" srcset="/static/img/page_edit_screenshot@2x.jpg 2x, /static/img/page_edit_screenshot@1.5x.jpg 1.5x" style="width: 309px; height: 211px;" />
<div class="description">
<h2>It's easy!</h2>
<p>
Adding to LocalWiki is as easy as making a Facebook post – contributing <a href="/main/Help" target="_blank">requires no technical knowledge</a> or academic background. We have no complicated rules for how to format a page, or even <a href="/main/What_can_I_write_about%3F" target="_blank">what to write about</a>: if it's useful or beautiful to your community, then it's worth sharing!
</p>
</div>
